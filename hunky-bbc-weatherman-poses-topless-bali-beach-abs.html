Hunky BBC weatherman drives fans wild as he poses topless on Bali beach and shows off rock-hard abs

BBC weatherman Tomasz Schafernaker left fans in awe after after posting a series of hot holiday snaps.

The presenter typically hosts the weather whilst wearing a suit and tie - but Tomasz looked very different posing topless on a beach abroad.

Tomasz, who is a British-Polish meteorologist for the Met Office, could be seen showing off his toned physique in a candid photo where he sported colourful shorts.

Posting to Twitter, he wrote: "Saying 'bye' to #Bali and #GiliTrawangan#Indonesia. What a vibrant place. Will come again! Cold back home? Arriving in shorts…"

The former Celebrity MasterChef contestant enjoyed a dreamy backdrop of sea, sand and green mountains in the images.

Tomasz's fans flocked to the comments to share their love for the weatherman, with one writing: "It was cold - but it’s damn HOT now!"

The star has been keeping his followers updated via his Twitter account on his exotic trip.

He even documented the jaw-dropping weather whilst in Bali, writing to Twitter: "It’s not often that you get to experience a thunderstorm AND an #earthquake at the SAME TIME.

"Like something exploded deep underground & briefly jolted us upwards. Somewhat unsettling but interesting.

"Restaurant staff were even startled for a sec. I won’t forget this 'quakestorm'."

Tomasz has previously raised eyebrows over his appearance, after refusing to cut his lockdown hair-do despite it being likened to a 70's porn-star trim.

The weather host also notoriously caused controversy in 2010 when he flipped the bird at BBC news presenter Simon McCoy, whilst thinking he was off camera.

The star was then demoted to an off camera job just weeks later - yet despite this he was named Best TV Weather Presenter at the TRIC (Television and Radio Industries Club) Awards in the same year.
