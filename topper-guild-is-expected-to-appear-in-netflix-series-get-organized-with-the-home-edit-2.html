Topper Guild Is Expected To Appear In Netflix Series Get Organized With The Home Edit 2

TikTok star Topper Guild is all ready to appear in “Get Organized with The Home Edit 2” on Netflix alongside proprietors of the home organizing service, The Home Edit. The show begins on April 1, 2022. Host Clea Shearer and Joanna Teplin will assist the social media celebrity in simplifying his room and living in clutter-free surroundings for improved usefulness and productivity.

All about Topper Guild

Public figure Topper Guild, renowned for his wacky pranks and comedic routines, will soon be seen alongside Clea Shearer and Joanna Teplin, the presenters of Get Organized with The House Edit 2 helping him simplify his home and take control of his stuff. The social media star got famous on TikTok after his brother advised testing the app as everyone was on it. He “tried it for a week” by sharing his pranks and comedy videos on the app but gave it up owing to fewer followers.

However, he began sharing the videos again on the app after his brother handed him “this book called The Dip.” Speaking on how the book inspired his choice to attempt TikTok again, he told ONE37pm:

“And essentially the book’s about pushing through the days when you don’t want to do it. And recognizing that even though you love something, sometimes you’re not going to enjoy it, but you just have to remember that you love it.”

He added:

“And then I went back on, TikTok and promised myself I was going to upload three times every single day for two weeks and then I hit one popular video.”

After this, the celebrity began publishing regular videos and quickly became one of the most famous content providers on the network with a few of viral films. Guild has “28,000,000+ on TikTok and 6,000,000+ on YouTube,” according to his Instagram bio.

About “Get Organized with The Home Edit 2”

The show’s first season began in the autumn of 2020 with the proprietors of the home organizing business The Home Edit, Clea Shearer and Joanna Teplin. The first season featured several renowned A-list clientele, including Neil Patrick Harris, Reese Witherspoon, Eva Longoria, and Khloe Kardashian.

Following the same pattern, the show’s second season will have Chris Pratt, Katherine Schwarzenegger, Kelsea Ballerini, Winnie Harlow, Drew Barrymore, Danielle Brooks, Topper Guild, Kevin Hart, and Tyler Hubbard. The program will release on Netflix on April 1, 2022.
