Celebrities Showing Legs In Bodysuits: Photos Hollywood Life

Bodysuits are perfect when you want to tuck your shirt into your pants, but want a seamless look that doesn’t crumple up around the waistband. But while we typically see the one-piece styled with skirts, shorts, jeans and other bottoms, plenty of stars have opted to wear them solo for optimum leg exposure. This look is a go-to for performers like Halsey and Cardi B while they slay the stage, but we’ve also seen it on red carpets and even out in public! 

If you’re looking to rock a one-piece as streetwear, rompers are usually the way to go – but bodysuits are definitely more daring. Taylor Swift straddled the line between romper and bodysuit when she rocked a blue leotard by Mary Katrantzou with letters plastered all over it to the MTV Video Music Awards on Aug. 24, 2014. She paired the long-sleeved one-piece with maroon heeled booties for the event in Los Angeles. Ahead, you’ll see how even more of your favorite stars have styled bodysuits on their own!
