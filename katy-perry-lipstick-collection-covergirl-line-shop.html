Katy Perrys Lipstick Collection Shop Her New CoverGirl Line Hollywood Life

Katy Perry’s makeup is always glamorous and full of bold colors, so of course we’re jumping at the opportunity to wear lipstick created by Katy herself. Her collaboration is centered around 13 creamy and moisturizing shades with a semi-matte finish — because, according to COVERGIRL, Katy was on a “quest to find a perfect matte.”

The entire COVERGIRL Katy Kat line, from the rich colors down to the name of the shades, has Katy’s name written all over it. Everyone will hear you roar when you wear shades with names like “Magenta Minx,” “Perry Panther” and “Coral Cat.” We personally can’t wait to get our hands on “Kitty Purry!” The shades of crimson, berry, coral, pink and black are all infused with shea butter, leaving your pout covered in an intense coat of hydrating color.

Katy Perry’s Lipstick Collection — How To Pre-Order
