Who is Herman Moore:

Herman Moore is a famous Football Player. He was born on October 20, 1969 and his birthplace is Linwood, NJ. Herman is also well known as, Former NFL wide receiver who was a four-time Pro Bowler as a member of the Detroit Lions from 1991 to 2001. During his time with the Lions, he set franchise records in receptions (670), receiving yards (9,174) and receiving touchdowns (62), but the latter two marks would be surpassed by another standout receiver, Calvin Johnson.

Herman is originated from United States. From 1991 to 1998, he and the legendary Barry Sanders fueled the Detroit Lions offense.
