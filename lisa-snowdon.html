Lisa Snowdon- Wiki, Biography, Age, Height, Net Worth, Husband

Lisa Snowdon is a British television and radio presenter, as well as a fashion model, best known for hosting the Living TV reality show “Britain’s Next Top Model” from 2006 to 2009. She co-hosted Capital Breakfast on “Capital London” from 2008 to December 18, 2015. She was confirmed as a contestant for the sixteenth series of I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here! in November 2008. She was crowned the winner of the seventeenth season of “Celebrity Masterchef” in September 2022.

Lisa Snowdon was born on January 23, 1972, in Welwyn Garden City, Hertfordshire, England, the United Kingdom. Her nationality is British, and her ethnicity is British-White. She is the daughter of Nigel Snowdon, an insurance salesman, and Lydia Snowdon, a hairdresser. She is the eldest of two sisters. Lisa went to the Sir Frederic Osborn School. She was awarded a scholarship to the Italia Conti Academy of Theatre Arts. She tried out for the “Minipops.” After gaining too much extra height and her parent’s divorce when she was 17, she stayed in London and briefly worked as a dance and yoga teacher. As of 2023, she is 51 years old.
