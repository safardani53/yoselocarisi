Who is John Roebling:

John Roebling is a famous Engineer. He was born on June 12, 1806 and his birthplace is Germany. John is also well known as, Remembered best for designing the Brooklyn Bridge, this 19th-century civil engineer helmed numerous other major projects, including the Niagara Falls Suspension Bridge; the Allegheny Bridge in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania; and the John A. John is originated from Germany. Roebling and Golden Gate Bridge engineer Joseph Strauss were both responsible for landmark American suspension bridges.
