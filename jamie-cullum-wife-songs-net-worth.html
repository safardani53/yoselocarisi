Who is Jamie Cullum? Height, wife, biggest songs and net worth

JAMIE Cullum made a leap from singing to acting when he starred as the Club/Wedding Singer in The Halcyon.

But where else would you know the jazz singer from and what has he been up to lately?

Who is Jamie Cullum?

Jamie Cullum is known to most as a singer/songwriter and jazz musician.

His height measurement stands at 1.64cm.

During his career, the 40-year-old is mostly seen on his piano as he performs - but he can also play a selection of other instruments including drums and guitar.

He has been given a slew of awards and nominations for his work - including two BRIT awards nods for Best Male Artist and Best Live Act in 2005.

His first major award was the Rising Star gone at the British Jazz Awards.

He took part in the BBC's Biggest Weekend this summer.

What is Jamie Cullum's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Jamie is worth an estimated $12 million, which is equivalent to £9.7 million.

Most of those earnings would have been accumulated from his music sales, having released a whopping 8 albums to date.

His sophomore record, titled Pointless Nostalgic, became a nationwide bestseller, while 2003's Twentysomething was named the top-selling record by a jazz artist in the UK.

With his popular hits, Jamie is never far from the stage as he tours around the world for what is believed to be another generous fee.

On March 9, 2020, he will kick off his latest tour at the Bord Gáis Energy Theatre in Dublin before concluding the European leg on May 22 at the Musikkens Hus in Aalborg.

Who is his wife Sophie Dahl?

Jamie has been married to English model Sophie Dahl since 2010, and they have two daughters.

Lyra was born in March 2011, and Margot was born in March 2013.

Sophie is the daughter of legendary children's author Roald Dahl and American actress, Patricia Neal.
