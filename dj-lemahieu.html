Who is DJ LeMahieu:

DJ LeMahieu is a famous Baseball Player. He was born on July 13, 1988 and his birthplace is Visalia, CA. DJ is also well known as, Second baseman who debuted for the Chicago Cubs in 2011, then was traded to the Colorado Rockies in 2012. He won a Gold Glove Award with the Rockies in 2014 and an NL batting title with the team in 2016.

DJ is originated from United States. He joined a middle infield led by star shortstop Troy Tulowitzki in Colorado.
