Two huge Channel 5 shows return to screens TONIGHT with beloved hosts making comebacks

TWO major Channel 5 shows will be returning tonight in a back-to-back bonanza.

Both Jane McDonald and Susan Calman will be on screens to front their own travel and holiday shows in order to give fans at home a little bit of sunshine during the winter months.

TV legend Jane will be heading off to ultimate holiday destination Tenerife for her latest adventure.

Titled The Canary Islands with Jane McDonald, she will head to the holidaymakers island first before undertaking some island-hopping in order to find a slew of hidden gems.

In tonight's episode, Jane will make herself at home in the popular tourist resort of Playa de las Americas - known for its vibrant party life as well as its family friendly hotels.

Jane will go on a cable car tour of a mountain on the North side of the island in order to push her out of her comfort zone as well as take in the stunning views from the top of Mount Teide.

Her other activities will see her dine in a traditional Canarian eatery as well as visit a banana plantation with some fellow guests.

Finally, she will top off her day with a visit to the world famous water park, Siam Park.

Jane will indulge in going on the super fast water slides alongside some fellow holidaymakers and explore the finest attraction Tenerife has to offer.

Elsewhere, Scottish comedian Susan is kicking off another edition of her Cruising with show to Mexico.

Kicking off in California, Susan will be doing some celeb spotting as she heads to Hollywood.

Enjoying a whistle stop tour of an older part of Tinseltown, she soon travels to Long Beach to board her ship.

Before arriving in Mexico, Susan sets up a series of challenges and games for her and the ship's guests as she attempts to not waste a second of time and cram in as much as possible during her break.
