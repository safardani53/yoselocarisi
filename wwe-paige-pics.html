WWE Star Paige: Pics Hollywood Life

Saraya-Jade Bevis, more popularly (and professionally) known as Paige, is an English TV personality and retired professional wrestler.

Quite impressively, she is a two-time Divas Champion and the inaugural NXT Women’s Champion, who has been signed with WWE since 2011, although she didn’t make WWE’s main roster until April 2014. 

Before making it big in the world of wrestling, Paige worked as a bouncer and bartender at her parents’ bar all while learning the great tricks of the trade from them (in case you didn’t know, she is the daughter of professional wrestlers Patrick Bevis and Julia Hamer-Bevis). 

She inevitably made her wrestling debut at the tender age of 13 when she competed through the World Association of Wrestling, under the ring name Britani Knight. She then went on to hold several championships on the independent circuit within Europe.

Unsurprisingly, by the age of 21, she became one of the youngest champions ever in the history of wrestling.
