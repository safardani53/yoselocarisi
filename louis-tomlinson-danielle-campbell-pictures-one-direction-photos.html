In case you’ve been living under a rock and don’t know who Louis Tomlinson is, he was a member of iconic English boy band One Direction (nickname: 1D). The band formed in London, England in 2010 through the hit TV show X Factor, and instantly gained international stardom and critical acclaim.  

Members Harry Styles, Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Zayn Malik, and Louis Tomlinson effortlessly swooned the hearts of millions around the world with their brilliant voices and charming looks. 

Some famous people the boys have particularly swooned are their girlfriends. Harry notably dated Taylor Swift for a bit, Niall dated Hailee Steinfeld, Liam dated Cheryl Ann Tweedy, Zayn dated Gigi Hadid, and last but not least, Louis dated Danielle Campbell. Louis and the American actress first met in November 2015 and then remained together for nearly a year.
