Celebrity Hunted 2023 cast: Who is taking part in the Channel 4 show?

CELEBRITY Hunted is back with a brand new batch of famous faces, including stars of Strictly Come Dancing and This Morning.

The Channel 4 show is returning with ten new celebrities all hoping to evade capture by a crack team of Hunters.

Who is in Celebrity Hunted 2023 cast?

Celebrity Hunted is back for a brand new series, as part of Stand Up To Cancer.

The show sees 10 celebrities attempting to stay off the radar and remain at large for two weeks.

While they try to remain hidden, a crack team of Hunters use intuition, determination and cutting-edge technical surveillance to try and track them down.

The Hunters, who are made up of former police officers, intelligence, and army personnel, aim to track down these famous fugitives by any means possible.

More on Celebrity Hunted

James Acaster and Ed Gamble

Comedians Ed Gamble and James Acaster kick off the Celebrity Hunted 2023 line-up,.

The pair's involvement in the series was revealed when Ed was forced to present his regular Radio X show in hiding from an undisclosed location.

While on air, he repeatedly called the Hunters "thick", prompting chief hunter Lisa Theaker to call into the show herself, telling the cheeky host he was "the worst fugitive we have ever had in the history of the Channel 4 show".

Ed agreed but teased: "We're absolutely terrible, and they’ve still not caught us. So what does that say about the Hunters?’'

He added: "To be fair, though, we did go for a nice meal in Shrewsbury when there was a helicopter chasing us, which wasn't the smartest idea..."

Katya Jones and Aimee Fuller

Strictly star Katya Jones is teaming up with former Olympic snowboarder Aimee Fuller.

Speaking to whattowatch.com, Katya said: "I'd like to do something outside of Strictly and it’s a chance for me to show my different sides and versatility.

"Lots of my friends are going through it with their family or close friends, so it's a good reason to do something worthwhile - and put yourself through hell at the same time!"

Nicola Thorp and Nikesh Patel

Soap star Nicola Thorp and her actor fiance Nikesh Patel are also among those taking part.

The acting couple will be hoping to outsmart the Hunters, as they try to evade capture.

Actress Nicola is best known for playing Nicola Rubenstein on Coronation Street.

Saffron Barker and Bobby Seagull

YouTuber Saffron Barker teams up with TV mathematician Bobby Seagull.

The pair will try to remain at large for as long as possible, without going undetected.

Saffron is best known for her YouTube channel and taking part in Strictly in 2019.

While clever clogs Bobby shot to fame on University Challenge.

Nik Speakman and Eva Speakman

TV therapists Nik and Eva Speakman complete the line-up.

The couple are best known for their work on This Morning.

The pair has studied human behaviour together for over 20 years and run their own therapy business helping people overcome a range of debilitating psychological issues.

When is Celebrity Hunted 2023 on Channel 4?

Celebrity Hunted kicks off tomorrow (March 28, 2023).
