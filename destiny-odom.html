Destiny Odom Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Bio:
Destiny Odom (born on August 6 1998) is the daughter of former NBA star, Lamar Odom. Destiny’s mother, Liza Morales, met Lamar while they were both attending King High School in Queens, New York. Liza and Lamar began a relationship and in 1998, the two welcomed their first child — Destiny —  to the world. After Lamar and Liza split, Destiny developed a positive relationship with her soon-to-be-stepmother, Khloe Kardashian. The relationship soured after Lamar and Khloe filed for divorce.

Best Known For:
Destiny Odom is best known as the daughter of Lamar Odom and Liza Morales, Lamar’s relationship before he married Khloe Kardashian.

Personal Life:
Destiny has two younger siblings. Lamar Odom Jr. was born in 2002. Their youngest brother, Jayden, died from Sudden Infant Death Syndrome in 2006. Jayden was just six months old. In 2013, Destiny trashed Khloe and Lamar’s marriage by saying “no one thought they were going to last” and she was glad that she had her dad all to herself again.  She has been very vocal about her disdain for her grandfather, Joe Odom. Destiny’s mother, Liza, said she did the reality show, Starter Wives, was to show her daughter her side of the story. Destiny is interested in fashion, fitness and has shown love to her family on her Instagram.
