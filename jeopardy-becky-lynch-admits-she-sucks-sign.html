WWE star Becky Lynch admits she 'sucks' at Jeopardy! with massive sign after dismal appearance on sh

WWE star Becky Lynch has embraced her historically bad performance on Jeopardy! during her latest pro wrestling match.

On Sunday night, she held up a fan’s giant sign reading: "BECKY SUCKS @ JEOPARDY."

Becky, 36, competed in the weekly celebrity edition of Jeopardy! earlier this month.

The RAW Superstar gave zero correct answers after 60 clues, a first in the long-running game show’s entire history.

Over the weekend, the red-headed Irish brawler beat Zoey Stark in Peoria, Illinois.

After the match, she shamelessly held up the fan-made sign seen above.

Becky embraced this may be the case, exclaiming: "It's true, it's true."

She then proudly stated: "But I'm good at fighting!"

BECKY GETS PUMMELLED

On November 15's episode, she competed against fellow celebs Rachel Dratch and Macaulay Culkin.

Lynch failed to get a correct answer out of the first 60 questions, which is a record low across the franchise, according to CBS Sports.

Jeopardy! rounds consist of 30 clues each, which means she went through the entire first round, and Double Jeopardy without getting a single clue right.

Thankfully, there is a new Triple Jeopardy! round in the weekly hour-long special, where Becky got two clues correct - and Final Jeopardy.

Still, no player has ever gone 60 clues without a single correct response, making Lynch, on paper, the worst-performing player in history.

In her defense, she's not from the states, and the episode was a record high in ratings, according to The Wrap.

The episode was helmed by Ken Jennings, 49, hosting the weekly special this season instead of a still missing Mayim Bialik, 47.

Mayim walked out of Jeopardy! after May's Writers Guild of America strikes and hasn't been on-screen since, even as the strikes have ended.
