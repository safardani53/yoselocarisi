Who Is Sky Daily? Five Things On Hulk Hogans Third Wife Hollywood Life

  • Hulk Hogan is considered to be the most recognizable former wrestler worldwide and was the most popular in the 1980s
  • Hulk announced his divorce and a new relationship with Sky Daily in 2022
  • Hulk and Sky were married in September 2023

Hulk Hogan is reportedly married! Hulk, 70, proposed to Sky Daily, 45, in July 2023, he confirmed to TMZ Sports on July 25, 2023, and the couple said “I do” on September 22, according to Daily Mail. The WWE champ said he asked Sky to be his wife while they were having dinner in a Tampa, Fla, restaurant and they then had a “low key” wedding in front of family and friends, minus Hulk’s daughter Brooke, two months later. The exciting news came over a year after Hulk confirmed their romance and his divorce from Jennifer McDaniel.

Hulk and the blonde beauty have kept their relationship under wraps, but they were spotted headed to Ric Flair‘s 74th birthday party in Florida in February 2023. They looked super happy, as Hulk had his arm around his woman. Find out more about Sky and her relationship with Hulk below.

Sky Is An Accountant And Yoga Instructor

Sky’s Instagram page lists her as an accountant and yoga instructor. The bio on her page also reportedly reads, “I help #SmallBusinessOwners plan & organize finances.”

When did Sky and Hulk start dating?

The lovebirds were first spotted together on Feb. 26, 2022, when they were backstage at a Bret Michaels concert. Hulk shared a video of the moment the day after and in the clip, he introduced the singer to Sky. Before the sighting, Hulk was known to be married to Jennifer since 2010, and rumors began to swirl. He took to Twitter to reveal he divorced in 2010 and confirmed that Sky was his girlfriend.

Yo Maniacs just for the record,the Facebook and Instagram posts are of me and my girlfriend Sky,I am officially divorced, sorry I thought everyone already knew,love my Maniacs4Life

— Hulk Hogan (@HulkHogan) February 28, 2022

“Yo Maniacs just for the record,the Facebook and Instagram posts are of me and my girlfriend Sky,I am officially divorced, sorry I thought everyone already knew, love my Maniacs4Life,” he wrote in the tweet.

Sky Is A Mother

Sky is reportedly the mother of three children, but their identities are unknown since she has never publicly announced their names outside of close family and friends.

Sky Appears To Be A Private Person

Sky’s social media accounts are all private and not much is known about her besides what she’s written in her bio. She’s also only been featured on Hulk’s social media accounts a few times and he hasn’t tagged her account in the posts.

Sky Is Hulk’s Third Wife

Before Hulk got into a relationship with Sky, he was married twice. His first marriage was to Linda Claridge from 1983 until 2009 and his second was to Jennifer from 2010 until 2021. He shares two children with Linda, including daughter Brooke and son Nick.
