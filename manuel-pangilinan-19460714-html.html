Manuel "Manny" Velez Pangilinan, also known by his acronym MVP, is a Filipino sports businessman and sponsor. He is the Chief Executive Officer and Chief Executive Officer of First Pacific Limited, a Hong Kong-based management and investment firm operating in the Asia-Pacific region. Pangilinan is also the CEO of First Pacific in charge of corporate investments in the Philippines, such as Metro Pacific Investments Corporation, PLDT and Philex Mining Corporation. He is the honorary chairman of Samahang Basketbol ng Pilipinas and its first president, serving two consecutive terms from 2007 to 2018.
