Sean Fujiyoshi Biography

Sean Fujiyoshi is a famed Japanese-American editor, actor, and producer. He is accompanied by his friend, YouTuber, and comedian, Ryan Higa at Ryan Higa Production Company. He is most popular for his YouTube channel Niga Haga and a feature film .

Early Life and Education

Sean Fujiyoshi was born on 3 August 1991, in Los Angeles, California, USA. He, together with his two nephews, was brought up by his elder sister. There is no information regarding his parents.

Fujiyoshi did his schooling from Waiakea High School. From 2009 to 2013, he pursued graduation degree from the University of Portland in Mechanical Engineering.

In the Portland University, Sean was an active member of the Hawaii Club and had participated in the sports.

Career, Net Worth, And Salary

The popular YouTuber Fujiyoshi, accompanied by his high-school friend Ryan Higa, started their career by filming comedy home-videos and uploading on YouTube. They later established their own Ryan Higa Production Company and launched the ‘YouTube’ Channel, ‘nigahiga’ in July 2006.

The YouTube channel is known for skits, rants, and music-video parodies. The short video series ‘How to be high rated and trended' has over 21 million views.

Achieving the huge success on YouTube, they stepped in a feature-length independent movie . The video series has more than a million viewers.

As of 2019, his channel has over 20 million subscribers and more than 3 billion viewers.

Amid their YouTube career, Sean and Ryan also focused on their education. Ryan enrolled at a college in Las Vegas whereas Sean moved to California with his girlfriend to pursue an engineering degree. During the course of time, Ryan continued to manage the ‘nigahiga’ YouTube channel.

After completion of education, the duo was back again. Sean appeared in the ‘nigahiga’ music video, ‘Bromance.’ In the video, they have featured a famed ‘YouTube’ personality Chester See. Sean became the editor, cinematographer, and producer of ‘Nigahiga’ in June 2013.

Sean worked for Ryan’s primary and secondary YouTube channels as a side-contractor. He managed all aspects of film production, from pre-production to post-production. At the same time, he continued his regular appearance in ‘nigahiga’s videos.

Alongside Sean worked on his engineering projects. He designed and tested an electric generator and produce electrical power which led his team the award for the ‘most outstanding Senior Design Project’ in 2013.
