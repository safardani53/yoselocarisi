Megan Fox appears to label fiance Machine Gun Kelly a narcissist in new book despite attending

MEGAN Fox has seemingly called out her fiance, Machine Gun Kelly, in her new book.

The Hollywood actress' book titled Pretty Boys Are Poisonous was released on Tuesday.

In one poem titled You Keep Telling Me It Was A Secret, Megan, 37, appeared to speak about MGK - real name Colson Baker, 33.

Megan and MGK revealed on social media that they were engaged on January 11, 2022, where she showed off her stunning emerald and diamond ring.

She seemingly discusses their rollercoaster relationship as they were hit with cheating and separation rumors.

In her poem, the supermodel wrote: "That you would never hurt me on purpose that you’re just too young to have known better but what’s the difference between manslaughter and murder? I'm still dead either way - a 32-year-old narcissist quantifies his crimes."

MORE ABOUT HER BOOK

On Monday, The U.S. Sun exclusively revealed more information about her stories - that isn't for the fainthearted.

On the front cover, a picture of a snake slithering through a pair of lips with a piercing on it was illustrated by Japanese illustrator Audrey Kawasaki.

In it, she discusses a wide range of topics including sex, abuse, rape, and murder in more than 70 poems.

In the prologue, the brunette beauty said that has always believed that she is a “sacrificial lamb” for whichever “beautiful, broken, self-absorbed idiot” is hunting her down.

Megan’s reference to her heart being broken by a “self-absorbed idiot” will prompt speculation on who she is referring to.

Describing herself as a “hopelessly romantic open wound of a human,” she adds: “But then one day it happened. One of said idiots finally broke me.”

Megan explains it was then that the poems featuring feelings of “isolation, torment, self-harm, desperation, longing, restlessness, rage, and general anguish” poured out of her.

It includes poems titled "rape," "funerals are for lovers," "why i wish i was gay," and "to marry an arsonist."

In one poem titled “it’s giving patrick bateman” - a reference to the serial killer protagonist of American Psycho - Megan writes: “Your love leaves bloodstains on my bedsheets.”

In yet another poem, she imagines a werewolf’s attempts to apologize for devouring her, blaming it on “family curses and sexual abuse.”

BEING SUPPORTIVE

Despite the rumors, Megan was recently spotted supporting her man at one of his concerts in Brazil.

In some new photos taken over the weekend, Megan sported short red hair while ditching her pants.

The Confessions of A Teenage Drama Queen star wore a loose-fitting beige blazer and skinny sunglasses.

On her feet, she wore a pair of thigh-high snakeskin boots.

HER PAST RELATIONSHIPS

In the past, Megan was linked to 7th Heaven star David Gallagher when they dated from 2003 to 2004.

When they broke up, Megan had an on and off again relationship with Beverly Hills, 90210 star Brian Austin Green.

In 2006, the two got engaged but briefly broke it off in 2009.

A year later, they got married.

The couple then split again in 2015 before calling it quits altogether in December 2019.

"I will always love her," Brian said on his podcast following their final split. "And I know she will always love me and I know as far as a family, what we have built is really cool and really special.

"It sucks when life changes and something that you're used to, that you've been doing for 15 years, you try and not get rid of but you change. There's the unknown aspect . . . there's that pit in my stomach . . . I really don't want Megan and I to be at odds . . . she's been my best friend for 15 years and I don't want to lose that."

Their divorce was later finalized in 2021, however, they will always be linked by their children, Noah, Bodhi, and Journey, with whom they share joint custody.
