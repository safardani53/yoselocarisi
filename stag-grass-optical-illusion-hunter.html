Everyone can see the stag leaping through the grass - you have eyes of a hunter if you spot the dog

A CLEVER optical illusion challengers viewers to find a dog hiding among a forest scene.

The brainteaser features a stag leaping through the grass, and only those with eyes of a hunter can spot a second animal in less than 10 seconds.

Optical illusions and puzzles are good for improving cognitive function and memory.

They help you flex your brain while challenging you to think outside the box.

This particular brainteaser shows a detailed sketch of a stag prancing in the mountainous wilderness.

He's surrounded by branches and trees, and has his two legs in the air as he hops along.

But, hidden within the majestic scenery is a dog, and it's your task to find him.

Here's a hint: try scanning individual sections rather than looking at the sketch as a whole.
