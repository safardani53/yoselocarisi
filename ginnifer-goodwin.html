Ginnifer Goodwin’s Age & Early Life

Ginnifer Goodwin was born Jennifer Michelle Goodwin on May 22, 1978, in Tennessee, United States. Tim Goodwin, the proprietor of a recording studio, is her father’s name. Linda is the name of her mother, who worked for FedEx. Ginnifer attended St. Mary’s Episcopal School before graduating from Lausanne Collegiate School.

She then attended Hanover College for a year to study and major in drama. Boston University awarded her a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree. She went to Shakespeare Institute after graduating from university. She was awarded the ‘Acting Shakespeare Certificate’ by the Royal Academy of Dramatic Arts. Goodwin received the Bette Davis Foundation’s ‘Excellence in Acting: Professional Promise Award’ upon graduation. She is of English descent.

Career

Ginnifer Goodwin made her professional debut in 2001 with the NBC television series Law & Order. She played Diane Snyder in the television series Ed from 2001 to 2003. Goodwin rose to prominence after portraying Maya in the Comedy Central TV film Porn ‘n Chicken. She provided the voice for seven episodes of Robot Chicken in 2005. Goodwin played Margene Heffman in the television series Big Love from 2006 until 2011. She subsequently had significant parts in TV films and programs such as Five, Electric City, and Who Do You Think You Are? The Twilight Zone, Killing Kennedy, and more films.

She has provided voice work for SpongeBob SquarePants and Sofia the First, in addition to acting. Between 2011 and 2018, she portrayed Mary Margaret Blanchard and Snow White on ABC’s fantasy drama series Once Upon A Time. Goodwin was later featured in I Am Somebody’s Child: The Regina Louise Story, Heartstrings, and Why Women Kill in 2019. She has also worked in cinema and television. In 2003, she made her film debut in Mona Lisa Smile.

Ginnifer portrayed Cathy Feely in Win a Date with Tad Hamilton the next year. Day Zero, Walk The Line, Birds of America, A Single Man, He’s Just Not That Into You, Ramona, and Beezus are among the films in which she has appeared. She appeared in Take Me Home Tonight and Something Borrowed in 2011. Goodwin provided the voice of Fawn in the 2014 film ‘Tinker Bell and the Legend of the Neverbeast’. Later that year, she gave her voice to Disney’s successful film Zootopia. She provided the voice for the 2015 video game Disney Infinite 3.0.

Ginnifer Goodwin Husband & Marriage

Who is Ginnifer Goodwin’s Husband? Ginnifer Goodwin has a husband. On April 12, 2014, she married actor Josh Dallas in California. She and Josh began dating during the production of Once Upon a Time in 2011, and they married in October 2013. This couple has two sons: Oliver Finlay Dallas, born in May 2014, and Hugo Wilson Dallas, born in June 2016. Ginnifer was previously engaged to actor Joey Kern in December 2010, however the couple called it quits in May 2011.
