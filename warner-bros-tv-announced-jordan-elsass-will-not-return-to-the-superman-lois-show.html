Jordan Elsass played Superman and Lois Lane in Superman & Lois Lane, and more information

Elsass played Trip Richardson in the program, which follows a family in Ohio in the 1990s. He also played Tyler Young in Amazon Prime Video’s adolescent drama series Panic. More information about Superman and Lois
