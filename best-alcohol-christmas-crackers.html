8 Best alcohol Christmas crackers 2021: from gin to salted caramel vodka

We know it can be a little tedious to pull a cracker with friends and family and receive a funny dad joke or riddle, as well as another bottle opener.

Instead, why not spice up your Christmas with booze-filled crackers that are sure to offer a little extra holiday happiness to everyone?

We've got you covered with a variety of alcohol crackers that will cater to everyone.

They are perfect as a Christmas gift for any adult, these elegantly-packed Christmas crackers include a sample bottle of alcohol and usually a joke and crown too - you can choose to have them all of the same type, from top notch gins to salted caramel flavoured vodka, or mix and match them.

Here's our selection of the best alcohol Christmas crackers.
