Jillian Michaels Poses Nude For Shape Magazine: Pictures

It’s official—Jillian Michaels is in really, really good shape. The fitness guru bared it all in Shape magazine’s 2014 July/August issue, posing nude and revealing a seriously toned bod.

The Biggest Loser trainer, who looks equally as fit on the cover in a gray tank and red bikini bottoms, revealed she feels better about herself now in her 40s than she did in her 20s.

“When I think back on my 20s and 30s, I look better now than I ever have. Yes, I’m older but I’m also wiser and that’s a more intrinsic type of beauty,” the 40-year-old told the mag.

Michaels regularly hits the gym to stay in shape, but she adds, it’s important for woman to be strong for their overall confidence.

“It’s important for women to have overall strength, because when you feel physically powerful, it transcends into every facet of your life,” she said.

The mom to daughter Lukensia, 4, and son Phoenix, 2 (with partner Heidi Rhoades) added despite looking every inch of perfection to us, she still has some imperfections.
