Bride ridiculed after wearing 600 Jacquemus straw hat on her wedding day and its SO big gue

WHEN it comes to picking an outfit for the big day, there's no surprise that most brides want to ensure they are the centre of attention.

And one model bride ensured all eyes were on her by wearing a giant Jacquemus straw hat that costs £600 and covered her face and shoulders.

Posted to Facebook group 'That's it, I'm wedding shaming', a woman explained she wanted to 'shame awful rich people weddings' because 'money only emboldens the ridiculous'.

She shared pictures of Danish actress and 'It' girl Emma Rosenzweig Leth, who said 'I do' in the oversized hat as part of a lavish wedding ceremony last summer.

The woman wrote: "I am always so mad when I can tell someone with more money that they know what to do with is like, 'I'm going to be quirky', while others bankrupt themselves to pull it off.

"The hat is so obviously absurd that I couldn't stop laughing at these pictures when I first saw them."

While walking down the aisle, the hat had to be flapped backward over bride's head to stop her face from being masked and ruining the wedding experience for guests.

Instagram girl Emma paired the straw accessory with a couture lace dress that the anonymous shamer begged for her to 'at least wear pretty panties underneath'.

And while plenty of fashion fans took to Emma's social media to dub the look 'gorgeous' and ask where they could get their own hat, many on the Facebook group agreed the look was 'overkill'.

One woman joked: "This is me when leaving the house without sunscreen on."

While another added: "So weird. That would actually be a beautiful dress with a plain white slip underneath and of course, a regular sized hat."

"The nude lace dress with the white set is okay," a third agreed. "But the hat is definitely overkill. It's just totally 'what the f***'.

"Did she marry herself?" concluded a man. "Where the f*** is the groom? Out foraging for appropriate undergarments and a normal hat for his new wife?"
