LPBW's Matt Roloff's dad slammed as an 'idiot' for suggesting his son and girlfriend have 'double we

LITTLE People, Big World fans slammed Matt Roloff's dad as an "idiot" after he suggested his son and girlfriend Caryn Chandler have a "double wedding" with ex-wife Amy Roloff and her fiancé Chris Mark.

The conversation took place during Tuesday's episode of the TLC show.

After Matt, 59, told his parents that he was working hard to build a new house, his father replied: "Are you guys going to get married? What's the deal? Who's the house for?"

The father added: "How can you have a home without a wife?"

Matt responded: "That may happen."

The reality star's dad then jokingly suggested: "I think it's be perfect if Amy and Chris and you guys got married the same day… have a double wedding.

"It's the modern family.

"You guys can set the pace for the modern family."

Matt's mom disagreed and told the couple that they shouldn't share their wedding with Matt's ex-wife.

In an interview, Caryn said her friends always ask if they'll get married.

The 53-year-old said: "I always say, you know, we're just not thinking about that right now.

"Not to say we never will, but it's just not a priority.

"I like it just the way it is."

Fans took to Twitter to slam Matt's father's suggestion for a double wedding, with one person sharing a gif of Joe Biden stating: "It's hard to respond to something so idiotic."

Another fan noted: "The look of terror on Matt’s face when his dad mentioned 'double weddings.'"

A third fan agreed, writing: "The look on Matt’s face when his dad suggested a double wedding with Amy & Chris."

Matt and Amy, 56, were married from 1987-2016.

The former couple share four kids: twins Zachary and Jeremy, 30, Molly, 27, and Jacob, 23.

While Matt has moved on with Caryn, Amy is currently engaged to Chris, 55.
