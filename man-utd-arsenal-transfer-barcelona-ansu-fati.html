Man Utd and Arsenal face transfer blow as wantaway Barcelona star Ansu Fati is offered to Champions

BARCELONA wonderkid Ansu Fati has reportedly been offered to Paris Saint-Germain.

Manchester United and Arsenal have targeted the forward in previous transfer windows.

But they could miss out after superagent Jorge Mendes offered his client around.

According to Sports Zone, PSG and Atletico Madrid have been given the opportunity to sign the forward.

The report adds that Barca boss Xavi will not stand in the way of Fati's departure, while Joao Felix could move the other way.

But PSG are believed to be eyeing Lyon star Bradley Barcola as their number one target.
