Jamie Dimon is an American billionaire businessman. He is the chairman and chief executive officer of JPMorgan Chase, the largest bank in the Big Four group of the United States and previously a member of the board of directors of the Federal Reserve Bank of New York. Dimon was on the list of the 100 most influential people in the world in 2006, 2008, 2009 and 2011 by Time magazine. Dimon's net worth is estimated at $ 1.7 billion. Dimon is one of the few bank executives to become a billionaire, in part thanks to a $ 485 million stake in JPMorgan Chase. He received a salary envelope of $ 23 million for fiscal 2011, more than any other bank executive in the United States. However, his compensation was reduced to $ 11.5 million by JPMorgan Chase in 2012 after a series of controversial business losses amounting to $ 6 billion. Dimon received $ 29.5 million in fiscal 2017
