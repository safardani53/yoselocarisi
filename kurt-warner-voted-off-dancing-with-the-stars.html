Kurt Warner Voted Off Dancing With the Stars

While Monday’s episode of Dancing With the Stars was a bit of a blur, last night’s was a bit of a foregone conclusion. One of two people would be leaving us.

Those people were not Kyle Massey, Jennifer Grey or Brandy.The instant dance portion of the night helped them, but Kurt Warner and Bristol Palin suffered.

It was these low-scoring celebs who had to worry. When Brandy was placed in the bottom two with Kurt, it was over. The conspiracy theory is alive and well.

Kurt Warner and Anna Trebunskaya gave it a heck of a run.

Ridiculous as it is that Bristol Palin is somehow in the final four this season, the ousted NFL quarterback didn’t seem to be shocked by the outcome Tuesday.

“I think, obviously there was the possibility when you’re at the bottom two,” Kurt Warner said. “We came in a number of times this year when we were low and kind of expected that the possibility was there, and you prepare yourself for that.”

“I’m going to miss [Anna Trebunskaya],” added two-time NFL MVP Kurt. “You know, I mean, when you spend so much time with someone and build this relationship, you know, it’s going to be tough not seeing her every day.”

“You’re going to miss Monday nights,” joked partner Anna. “You’re going to miss going out there in the bright neon green with fringes on your pants.”

Did the voters get it right this week? Is the show rigged to keep Bristol on? Who are you pulling for among the final four now?
