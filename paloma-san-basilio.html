Who is Paloma San Basilio:

Paloma San Basilio is a famous World Music Singer. She was born on November 22, 1950 and her birthplace is Madrid, Spain. Paloma is also well known as, Legendary singer and actress who was awarded a Latin Grammy for Lifetime Excellence in Spanish music. She began her career in the 1970s and enjoyed decades of success before her retirement in 2013. She even represented her home nation in the 1985 Eurovision Song Contest. 

Paloma is originated from Spain. In 1994, she performed a duet concert with opera singer Jose Carreras.
