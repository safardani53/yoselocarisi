Who is Gzuz:

Gzuz is a famous Rapper. He was born on June 29, 1988 and his birthplace is Hamburg, Germany. Gzuz is also well known as, German rapper and member of the hip hop crew 187 Street Gang. He’s collaborated jointly with 187 member Bonez MC on the album High & Hungry, which became a top ten hit in Germany as well as their sequel, High & Hungry 2, which debuted at number one. His solo album titled Ebbe & Flut reached number two on the German charts.

Gzuz is originated from Germany. He and 187 specialize in the gangster rap genre like rap legend Tupac.
