PRIMARK fans are gushing over its new pyjama sets which are perfect for warm summer evenings.

The short and vest sets have been a hit with fruit-loving fashionistas, as they’re covered in mini limes, clementines, cherries and strawberries.

The cherry pyjama set, complete with lettuce hem shorts and a baby pink crop top, were the biggest hit.

The fast fashion retailer’s Cardiff store uploaded a TikTok showcasing the new nightwear, sparking a social media frenzy. 

“I’m going to cry if I don’t get some of those cherry pyjamas,” one woman said.

“The cherry pyjamas are everything,” another quipped.

One fashion fan even joked she wanted “to be buried” in the pyjamas, while others debated wearing the set to a Harry Styles concert.

Primark’s Lettuce Hem Cami and Shorts Pyjama Set are just £9 a pop and come in three designs - strawberries, clementines and limes.

“Made from a cotton blend, these pretty pyjamas will prove the perfect nightwear for those warmer nights,” bosses at the retailer said. 

“Coming as a two-piece set, they include a cami top and coordinating shorts that each rock a fun, fruit-themed print.
