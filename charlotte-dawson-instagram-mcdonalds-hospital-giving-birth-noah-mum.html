Charlotte Dawson eats McDonald's in her hospital bed straight after giving birth in throwback pic

CHARLOTTE Dawson kept it real straight after giving birth to her first baby Noah - with a slap-up McDonalds in her hospital bed.

The reality TV star, who chowed down an extra-hot Nandos in order to bring on her labour, finished off her journey to motherhood along the same fast-food theme.

Ex On The Beach personality Charlotte, 28, has now revealed the special moments after becoming a mum with a snap which showed her munching on a McDonalds chicken nugget and holding a pot of dip.

Both a burger and a carton of chips sat on her lap and she flashed a pout to the camera mid-meal.

The MTV star wore a pretty floral cardigan and her brunette locks tied back in a ponytail as she lay in her bed for the meal.

In her typically candid style, she wrote in her image caption: While some birds get bags and shoes for a push present ... I get Maccies and I was buzzin," before finishing off her post with a party Emoji icon.

The accompanying video showed little Noah, Charlotte's first child with rugby player Matt Sarsfield, lying peacefully in his cot as his mum enjoyed her tea.
