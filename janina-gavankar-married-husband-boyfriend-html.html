Janina Gavankar Family: If She Is Married With Husband Or Not, Find Here

A popular American actress, Janina Gavankar is notable for many aspects in the Hollywood business. Actress, vocalist, pianist, and orchestral percussionist are some of Janina's’ wide range of talents. She is famous for providing her voice to “FERO”, one of the lead characters in ‘Halo 5: Guardians” promo event - ‘Hunt The Truth’. Along with this, she has also acted in a number of films, web series, television programs, and theatrical plays. Her portrayal of Papi in ‘The L Word’, Luna Garza in ‘True Blood’, Meredith Bose on ‘The Mysteries Of Laura’ are some of her most notable performances amongst a list of many.

Not only does she act, but the Illinois native has sung and played marimba for several movies and television scores. She has also released a cover of Kanye West’s Song “ Love Lockdown.”  A graduate of the University of Illinois, Gavankar also has her fingers dipped in the field of technology. She is known for developing free templates for Hollywood artists to help them manage their presence on the online market.

Know About Janina’s Age, Family & Height

Janina Zione Gavankar was born on  29 November 1980 in Joliet, Illinois, USA. For a female, she stands at a decent height of 5’7” or 1.7 m. She is the first of two daughters to parents Pete Gavankar and Shan-De-Mohra. Even though her father has passed away, her June 2017 twitter post shows how much he meant to her and how much she misses him.
