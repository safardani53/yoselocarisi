Chelsea finally take the top spot in FIFA 23 Team of the Week

AFTER two 90+ cards last week, FIFA 23’s Team of the Week (TOTW) returns to cards rated in the 80s.

However, this week does retain the two double-boosted introduced last week.

Chelsea defender Thiago Silva has the highest-rated card this week with an 89 rating.

Argentinian player Paulo Dybala also has been boosted to an 89-rating.

These are the two highest-rated cards in FIFA Ultimate Team (FUT) this week, but there are several cards in the high 80s.

If you download the FIFA 23 companion web or mobile app, you can open FIFA Ultimate Team (FUT) packs on the go.

More in FIFA 23

This means you can start building your team ready to play in the game.

The TOTW cards will show up as special cards in FUT packs this week, with boosted stats.

James Ward-Prowse is one of the two rare double boosted-cards this week, with a five-point boost from 82 to 87.

Krépin Diatta is the other rare double boosted, with an unprecedented 12-point boost from 74 to 86.

This week’s team will be available via FUT packs until Wednesday, January 25, 5pm UK time.

After that, these boosted cards will only be purchasable via the transfer market, usually for high prices.

Here is a list of all the players in FIFA 23’s Team of the Week and their boosted stats:

  • 88 - Thiago Silva, Dybala
  • 87 - Ward-Prowse, Oshimhen, Traoré
  • 86 - Diatta, Joselu
  • 85 - Ramsdale, Rashford, Lejeune, David
  • 83 - Galeno, Pepe Reina, Kolašinac, Thuram, Roberton, Lookman, Pukki
  • 82 - Zerrouki
  • 81 - Kemen, Tesche
  • 79 - Cornette, Penrice
