Kellie Martin Photos Of The Actress Hollywood Life

Kellie Martin is an actress best known for playing Becca in the beloved series ‘Life Goes On,’ Lucy Knight on ‘ER,’ and Hailey Dean in the ‘Hailey Dean Mystery’ TV film series. In her earlier years, she also starred as Emily in 1989’s ‘Troop Beverly Hills.’ Kellie notably starred in the cult classic Lifetime movie ‘Death of a Cheerleader’ alongside Tori Spelling in 1994. She returned in the remake and played the role of an FBI agent. Kellie has appeared in a number of Hallmark movies and series over the years. The actress was nominated for an Emmy in 1993 for Outstanding Supporting Actress in a Drama Series for ‘Life Goes On.’ She was also nominated Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Drama Series at the 1999 SAGs for her performance on ‘ER.’ Kellie has been married to Keith Christian since 1999.
