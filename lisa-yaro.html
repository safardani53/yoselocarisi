Who is Lisa Yaro:

Lisa Yaro is a famous Pop Singer. She was born on February 8, 1985 and her birthplace is Nigeria. Lisa is also well known as, Grammy-nominated singer and songwriter who’s collaborated on popular tracks such as “Spark” and “Superwoman.” As an actress, she has recurred on the series Played by Fame and appeared in the 2018 film London Mitchell’s Christmas.

Lisa is originated from Nigeria. She executive produced the 2017 series Chosen Kin Origins which starred Levy Tran.
