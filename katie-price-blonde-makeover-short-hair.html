Katie Price shows off blonde makeover after revealing her 'real' short hair on TV

KATIE Price has undergone another mega hair transformation just days after revealing her natural short hair on TV.

The former glamour model, 42, stunned fans earlier this week when she ditched the wig and showed off her real hair during a segment on Steph’s Packed Lunch.

But today Katie switched up her look again for a trip to her local equestrian shop with daughter Bunny, six.

The telly personality decided to ditch the dark side and find out if blondes do really have more fun.

Katie rocked her new hairstyle with black jumper, matching pair of joggers, a pair of Nike Air Max and a Louis Vuitton cross body bag.

The mum-of-five added a pop of colour to her casual outfit with some neon pink nails and a face full of flawless make-up.

Bunny opted for a similar vibe to her mum as she stepped out in a black jumper with coloured stars placed down the sleeves, matching leggings and some horse riding boots.

The mother daughter duo were out and about buying some bits for the youngster's new pony.

Her new look comes after she appeared on the Channel 4 talk show donning a brunette bob.

Katie told host Steph McGovern, 38: "I ditched the hair extensions.

"I wear the wig sometimes - you'll see on my VT later I've got long blonde hair.

"This is my own hair, I'm trying to grow out. I'm just not used to it, I've never had short hair. I'm just sort of trying to get used to it."

Katie was appearing on the show after being signed up as one of Steph's Packed Lunch's reporters.

She presented a harrowing film about internet trolling - an issue close to her heart after son Harvey, 18, was abused online.
