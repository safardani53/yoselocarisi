Emilio Ramon Pelayo Ejercito III, commonly known as E.R. Ejercito and also known by his official on-screen names are Jorge Estregan, George Estregan Jr. and Jeorge "E.R." Ejercito Estregan, a Filipino actor and former Governor of Laguna from 2010 until he was removed from office in 2014. Before being elected Governor, he was Mayor of Pagsanjan, Laguna from 2001 to 2010. He is the son of actor George Estregan and nephew of former President and former Mayor of Manila Joseph Estrada. On May 27, 2014, the Electoral Commission overthrew Ejercito after allegedly overspending during the 2013 midterm election campaign. Three days later, his uncle, Joseph Estrada, gave a speech. admire you to resign. On April 5, 2019, he was convicted and sentenced to between 6 and 8 years in prison for signing an extraordinary insurance contract as mayor of Pagsanjan in 2008.
