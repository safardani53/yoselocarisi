M&amp;S is selling the most comfortable trousers that fit like a dream no matter your size -

FASHION fans are in a flurry over a pair of M&S trousers they say are the perfect fit.

One woman took to social media to show off the Marks & Spencer trousers branding them 'the most comfortable wide leg trousers'.

The woman who goes by S.Buckingham on TikTok was seriously impressed with the quality - and she's not the only one.

The fashion fan purchased them in cream and was thrilled to see they were super thick making them not see-through.

They also feature a buckle at the back of the waist so that you can cinch it in for the perfect fit to your body shape.

Not only are they comfy - but they're the perfect style to dress up or down to more formal events such as work or pair with a fancy top.
