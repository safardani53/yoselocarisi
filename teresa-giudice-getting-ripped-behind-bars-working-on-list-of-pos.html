Getting RIPPED Behind Bars, Working on List of Post Prison-Goals

She may not have gotten that prison kitchen job she was hoping for, but that doesn’t mean Teresa Giudice isn’t making the most of her time behind bars.

Us Weekly reports that Teresa is working out like crazy in the big house…and she’s already seeing results:

"She’s getting in the best shape of her life," a source tells the tabloid. "Every night she hits the gym and she’s been asking friends to send her workout videos."

Could the her desire to get jacked have anything to do with the rumors that Teresa is being targets by other inmates?

We may never know, but by the time she gets sprung, she’ll probably be a more intimidating table-tossing force than ever before.

And apparently it’s not just her body that Teresa is looking to improve:

"Teresa writes down everything she wants to do when she gets out," says the source. "She hopes to land the cover of a magazine and get her own cooking show."

Yes, we’ve heard rumors of a Teresa Giudice cooking show before. Hey, a little time in the clink didn’t hurt Martha Stewart’s career as a lifestyle guru. 

As an added bonus, when you’re taking kitchen tips from an ex-con, you might learn how to turn a rubber spatula into a deadly shiv!

Watch The Real Housewives of New Jersey online to remind yourself of just how little Teresa cooked when she was a free woman.
