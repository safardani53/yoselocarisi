Who is Donna Leon:

Donna Leon is a famous Novelist. She was born on September 29, 1942 and her birthplace is New Jersey. Donna is also well known as, American-born crime novelist and university professor. She became famous for her Guido Brunetti series of detective novels.

Donna is originated from United States. She and fellow detective fiction author Sara Paretsky were both recipients of the Crime Writers’ Association’s Silver Dagger Award.
