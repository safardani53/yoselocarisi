Harry Styles looks identical to Olivia Wildes ex Jason Sudeikis with huge moustache as hes up

AFTER being handed three nominations for the posh Ivor Novello Awards, Harry Styles appears to have undergone an arty makeover.

But in the process the singer has become a double of his girlfriend Olivia Wilde’s ex-husband, actor Jason Sudeikis.

Harry yesterday revealed a very bushy ’tache, and all I can see is Jason’s leading character in his hit TV show Ted Lasso.

I’m sure the copycat look is a coincidence, but if I was Olivia, I’d be whipping out a razor faster than you could say Gillette.

Harry showed off the new look on landing in the US with Olivia following a month’s holiday in Italy.

He now needs to work on his speeches for the Ivors, which celebrate excellence in song writing. He leads the nominations, alongside his co-writer Kid Harpoon.

The pair jointly get nods for Harry’s smash hit songs Watermelon Sugar and Adore You and are both up for the Songwriter of the Year gong.

Mnek, Kamille, Celeste and Jamie Hartman and AJ Tracey all in the running too.
