Demi Lovato Says Goodbye to &quot;Vagina Tattoo,&quot; Shows Off New Ink

Demi Lovato has given fans another reason not to do drugs.

The singer took to Instagram this week to show off her new tattoo, a large piece of permanent ink in the shape of a rose.

She included with it a confusing/hilarious caption that reads:

“Bye bye [kiss emoji] and hello [rose emoji].. Thank you SO MUCH to my brother @GEESPOTAT2… It looks beaaauutiful and I LOVE it!! #RIPvaginatattoo #kidsdontdodrugs.”

See, the former Disney star previously had a pink kiss tattoo near her wrist, which she had now had turned into the rose’s leaf.

Based on her own written words, it sounds as if Lovato got that pink kiss tattoo while high on some kind of substance – and it also sounds as if the pink kiss tattoo resembled a female private part.
