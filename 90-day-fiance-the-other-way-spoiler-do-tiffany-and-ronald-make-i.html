90 Day Fiance The Other Way Spoilers: Do Tiffany and Ronald Make It?

A week ago, TLC dropped the first trailer for Season 1 of 90 Day Fiance: The Other Way. It looks more than a little bonkers.

The show features six Americans and their significant others as the Americans consider moving overseas for love.

Since then, we have learned a few spoilers, including a great deal about Corey and Evelin.

But what about Tiffany and Ron?

Tiffany, a strikingly beautiful American woman, and Ronald, the South African man she met and fell in love with … before learning of his checkered past.

We now know whether she takes that major step in uprooting herself and her son for love.

What’s more is that we finally know which star from this season is (secretly) pregnant.
