Pics Of The Boxer Hollywood Life

Floyd Mayweather Jr. is a professional boxer. He began competing professionally in 1996 after winning bronze at the Olympics that summer. Floyd’s career lasted from 1996 until 2007, after which he took a short hiatus before returning from 2009-2015. In 2017, he made a comeback for a highly-anticipated fight against Conor McGregor, which he won, upping his professional boxing record to 50-0. This put him ahead of Rocky Marciano, who held the previous record of 49-0. Of Floyd’s wins, 27 were by knocking and 23 were by decision. In addition to boxing, Floyd has also done some work for the WWE, and appeared on ‘Dancing With The Stars, in 2007. Throughout his career, Floyd has also been involved in a few legal issues, and has had beef with celebrities like T.I., 50 Cent and more.
