Joe Rogan Latest News and Updates

Comedian, podcast host, and TV personality Joe Rogan started his career in comedy in the late '80s in Boston before moving to Los Angeles full-time in 1994. 

He began working for the UFC in 1997, and shortly after, he was cast as the host of Fear Factor in 2001. After stepping down as host in 2006, Joe continued to work in television until he started his podcast, The Joe Rogan Experience, in 2009.

In 2020, Joe signed a licensing deal with Spotify for $100 million, making his extremely popular podcast exclusive to the streaming platform. 

Joe is also a black belt in jiu-jitsu and the proud father and stepfather to three daughters.
