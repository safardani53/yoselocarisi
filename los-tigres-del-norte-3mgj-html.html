Norteño group from San Jose with origins in Mocorito, Sinaloa, Mexico that has released more than 50 albums in the 50 years between 1968 and 2018.

The group won the Best Mexican/Mexican-American Album Grammy for Gracias!...América...Sin Fronteras. They also won Grammy's for the albums Raíces, Tu Noche Con Los Tigres Del Norte, MTV Unplugged: Los Tigres Del Norte And Friends and Realidades (Deluxe Edition).
