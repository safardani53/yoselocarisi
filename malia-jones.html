Malia Jones Biography

Malia Jones is one of the famous models from the United States. She has also established herself as a surfer.

Early life

Malia Jones was from the middle-class family born on 27th March 1977 in Loma Linda, California, the United States of America to John Jones and Violet Jones-Medusky. Though she was born in Loma Linda, California, she spends most of her childhood days in Kailua, Hawaii.

She is an American and is of diverse descent.

Since her childhood days, she showed some quality in surfing and also in modeling.

Married life and divorces

Malia Jones has been married three times in her life. She was once married to Conan Hayes, they got married in 1999 and they continued their married life till 2007. But something went wrong in this married life so, they decided to dissolve their married life in 2007.

Soon after being divorced with her first husband, she then got married to Luke Stedman. He is a professional surfer from Australia and he has also won several individuals from his surfing career. They also had a child together.

Soon after getting divorced with him, she began dating Alex O'Loughlin. He is one of the renowned actors from Australia and he is popular in the world for portraying his role in the television series starring Scott Caan.

They also have a child together and they finally got married in 2014. She is happy to have him as her loving spouse. 

Career and net worth

When she was in her early teens she took part in her first major surfing competition. After that, she became the national champion when she was just fifteen years old.

As a model, she has appeared for commercials for many big names like Coca-Cola, American Express and Corona Beer to name a few.

Net worth

Her salary and net worth are assumed to be high. Her husband is also wealthy and has a net worth of $7 million.
