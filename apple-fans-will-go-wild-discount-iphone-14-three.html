Apple fans will love 140 discount on Three iPhone 14 plan

If you're hunting for an Apple phone and you've got your sights set high, head to the Reward Mobile website.

The provider has slashed the upfront cost of a £43-per-month iPhone 14 plan from Three to £0.

The iPhone 14 was released onto the UK market in September last year, and earned a glowing review from our tech editor Sean Keach.

Cheap this Apple mobile ain't, but thanks to the £140 price drop from Reward Mobile, it's sure as heck a lot cheaper than it was, with all costs now spread across the monthly bill.

It comes with a hefty 100GB of monthly data which should keep the average phone user more than happy.
