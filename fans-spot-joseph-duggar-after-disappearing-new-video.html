Duggar fans spot reclusive family member on Florida beach trip two years after disappearing from spo

ONE rarely-seen family member has been spotted in a new video shared by Joy-Anna Duggar to document a recent Florida beach trip.

Joy-Anna, 25, uploaded a YouTube video to share moments from the vacation, where her older brother Joseph Duggar, 28, made a cameo.

Joe was seen from the side as Joy-Anna panned the kitchen table in the vlog.

The house was full of other family members, including her husband Austin Forsyth, 29, as the Counting On star announced they were all heading to the beach for the day.

While Joseph was only shown for a few seconds in the vlog, he could be seen wearing a gray T-shirt, along with a matching hat and sunglasses placed on top.

One fan posted a screenshot of the YouTube video to an online thread, pointing out Joe's attendance on the family trip.

Fans also noted that the Duggar family patriarch, Jim Bob, 58, could be seen in the background of the shot as well.

PRIVATE DUGGARS

Joe and his wife, Kendra Duggar, 24, tend to keep things more private - having not shared any posts to their Instagram feed since November 2021.

Kendra and Joseph tied the knot in 2017 and already share three children: Garrett, five; Addison, three; and Brooklyn, two.

Last year, however, fans theorized that the couple might have welcomed a secret fourth child.

Joy-Anna posted a video of a family party on YouTube, where fans believe they spotted Joe and Kendra with a new baby.

Joe wore a baby in a carrier under his brown overcoat as he bounced up and down, while Kendra appeared to be seated on a couch with a baby in her lap.

Speculation arose again over the summer, as Kendra was seen holding a mysterious baby once more in another one of Joy-Anna's vlogs.

After being extremely active on social media about her pregnancies in years past, Kendra's fans wondered if she went silent because of her brother-in-law Josh's prison sentence.

Josh's child pornography investigation began in November 2019, followed by his arrest in April 2021.

Others, however, believed that they were simply protecting their children from public scrutiny.

One fan previously wrote on Reddit: "This is one of the only respectable things they’ve done. Not exploiting their children will make a world of difference for them growing up.

"Joe didn’t have a choice because his parents signed him up, and from there he just followed along."
