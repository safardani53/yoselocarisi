NICK Cannon's fourth baby mama Alyssa Scott busted out of her dress on her night out just weeks after she welcomed his seventh child last month.

Alyssa shared a few snapshots of herself posing in a sexy green dress on Instagram. 

The model had curled up her long blond hair. 

She also had on a handbag to go with her stunning green dress. 

The dress also had a v-cut that seemed to tease that Alyssa was braless. 

In her Instagram story, Nick's baby mama posted a shot from the back of her head, which flaunted her round earrings, the fancy rings on her fingers, bracelets, and gold necklace. 

Alyssa wrote: "Matching all this amazing leo energy floating around leos, geminis, and sag are top tier in my book."

While taking a snap of her feet, Alyssa jokingly captioned the Instagram story: "Tried to get a shoe shot but my circulation was struggling."

'AMAZING LEO ENERGY'

Earlier in June, Alyssa revealed she had given birth and she shared three snapshots. two of which showed her holding her newborn.

'FOR ETERNITY'

The Drumline actor also shared an adorable video with his four-year-old son Golden and daughter Powerful Queen, seven months, with Brittany Bell.
