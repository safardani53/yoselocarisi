The 8 best balloon pumps for stress-free party prep in 2022

CELEBRATIONS should be filled with joy, not light-headed dizziness. That's why you need to know about the best balloon pumps around.

Giving you a helping hand when inflating everyone's favourite floating plastic decorations, balloon pumps are a must-have when it comes to party prepping.

The best part? They're really affordable. That, and the fact that they save you so much time is enough to persuade anyone to invest.

Luckily, simple plastic balloon pumps can be had very cheaply from online party stores, though an electric balloon pump is a better idea if you’ve grand plans to tackle making a balloon garland or arch or a balloon table centrepiece.

We’ve rounded up a range of balloon pumps, both manual and electric for a quick and effective way to get your balloons inflated and the party started.

How to use a balloon hand pump

A balloon pump is a tool that allows you to inflate a balloon quickly and easily without needing to blow repeatedly.

A balloon hand pump operates with the hand movement, usually going up and down, allowing hair into the balloon.

Some balloon pumps also feature a "double-action" pump, meaning air is pushed into your balloons on both the up and down movements, making inflation even easier.
