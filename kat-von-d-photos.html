Photos Of The Tattoo Artist Hollywood Life

Queen of the ink! Katherine Von Drachenberg, more popularly known as Kat Von D, is a Mexican tattoo artist, model, musician, author, entrepreneur, and television personality. The tattooed beauty, who was born Mar. 8, 1982, is best known for appearing as a tattoo artist on the TLC reality television show ‘LA Ink.’ The show ran for four seasons, premiering in the United States on August 7, 2007, and ending on September 15, 2011.

After running her own successful tattoo shop she moved on to the world of makeup, creating her own vegan and animal cruelty-free collection. The star is also behind a range of vegan footwear.

Kat married Prayers singer Leafar Seyer, aka Rafael Reyes, in 2018. The couple shares a son Leafar Von Drachenberg Reyes, born in 2018.
