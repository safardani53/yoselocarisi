Survival of the Thickest Is Set To Released On Netflix

Survival of the Thinnest is an exciting new comedic drama series that will premiere exclusively on Netflix on July 13, 2023. The forthcoming Netflix series is based on Michelle Buteau’s autobiography of the same name. Buteau, together with Danielle Sanchez-Witzel, is also the series creator.

Mavis Beaumont, a very passionate and energetic African-American stylist, will be key to Survival of the Thickest. The series will follow the heroine as she attempts to remake her life on her own terms. On June 23, 2023, the streaming platform released the series’ official trailer.

Season 1 of Survival of the Thickest will consist of eight episodes.

The highly anticipated first season of the series will consist of eight episodes and will be published on July 13, 2023. All eight episodes will run at 3:00 a.m. Eastern Time (ET). Danielle Sanchez-Witzel, one of the show’s creators, has also acted as the series’ developer. Linda Mendoza, a well-known American television director, directed the series.

Netflix’s official summary for Survival of the Thickest, along with the show’s official trailer, reads as follows:

“The story revolves on Mavis Beaumont (Michelle Buteau). Mavis, who is black, plus-size, and newly unmarried (not by choice! ), is forced to rebuild her life as a failing stylist. With the support of her chosen family, a body-positive mindset, and a nice v-neck with some lip gloss, she’s determined to not just survive but flourish. Buteau’s outstanding volume of essays inspired the written comedy.”

The series’ official trailer and description provide fans with fascinating views and hints about what the series will bring to the table. The audience seems to be in for a pleasant and feel-good adventure as the main heroine Mavis embarks on a voyage of self-refection and self-love after having her heart shattered.

The new series will highlight the protagonist’s unwavering resolve to establish her footing in both her professional and personal lives as she works to accomplish her objectives and discover true love. The new series will also address vital subjects such as equality, body positivity, self-care, and others. Without question, the series will take viewers on an interesting and exciting adventure.
