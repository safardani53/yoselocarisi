Taylor Swift, Karlie Kloss Enjoy California Road Trip: See the Picture

Is there anyone Taylor Swift isn’t best buds with? The Grammy winner plays host to a series of A-List pals including Selena Gomez, Demi Lovato, Hailee Steinfeld, Sarah Hyland, and Lorde. 

And on March 5, Swift, 24, and Victoria’s Secret gal pal Karlie Kloss hit the Pacific Coast Highway for an all-girls road trip. Exploring California’s Pfeiffer Big Sur State Park, the friends posed for Instagram shots in the park’s giant forest. 

“Beach, forests, cliffs, Northern California, sand, flower picking. Best road trip ever,” Swift captioned one picture.

With breathtaking views and plenty of frolicking, the girls seemed to be having a ball visiting a bakery and running down the beach. 

Swift has spent time with several Victoria’s Secret models after performing at the annual fashion show in the fall.
