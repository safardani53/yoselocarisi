Dude Perfect: Who are the members?

DUDE Perfect is a popular comedy group that often does outrageous stunts in an attempt to break world records.

The group was formed at Texas A&M University back in 2009, and now fans want to know more about the members.

Who are the Dude Perfect members?

Dude Perfect was founded by five guys who were roommates while attending Texas A&M University.

"Five dudes, a camera and a friendly bet. That's how Dude Perfect was born," they state on their website.

Since their start, they have been able to amass a fortune, as well as millions of followers on YouTube.

The members include:

Garrett Hilbert

Garrett Hilbert has often been described as the "dad" of the group because he often keeps the group organized.

He hails from Plano, Texas, and was born on May 13, 1987.

Since 2011, Hilbert has been married to Kristen Hilbert and they now share three children, Owen, Iris, and Lincoln.

Garrett Hilbert

Tyler Toney has often been described as the face of the group due to his outgoing personality.

He hails from Prosper, Texas, and was born on March 24, 1989.

Toney also holds the record for the most solo battle wins at Dude Perfect and is the youngest member of the group.

Since 2011, he has been married to Bethany Toney and they now share three children, Barrett, Colton, and Rhett.

Cody Jones

Cody Jones is known as the "Tall Guy" of the group, measuring in at six-and-a-half feet.

He hails from Plano, Texas, and was born on October 9, 1987.

Since 2010, he has been married to Allison Jones and they now share three children, Landry, Lucy, and Easton.

Cory Cotton

Cory Cotton is the all-time leader in wheel spins and has been described as the "Michael Jordan of Unfortune."

He hails from The Woodlands, Texas, and was born on July 17, 1987.

Since 2016 he has been married to Amy Cotton, and they now share three children, Crew, Collins, and Charlie.

Coby Cotton

Coby Cotton is Cory's twin brother and is still waiting for his breakout year.

Over the past 12 years, he has only won four battles but is convinced his time will come.

Like Cory, he hails from The Woodlands, Texas, and was born on July 17, 1987.

Since 2017 he has been married to Aubrey Ellett and they now share one daughter, Carter Elisabeth.

What is Dude Perfect's net worth?

Due to Dude Perfect's success, the group has been able to amass a fortune over the years.

As of September 2022, the group is worth $50million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.
