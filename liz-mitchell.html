Who is Liz Mitchell:

Liz Mitchell is a famous Pop Singer. She was born on July 12, 1952 and her birthplace is Jamaica. Liz is also well known as, Famous for her work with the disco group Boney M, this dance, pop, and soul vocalist also released such solo albums as No One Will Force You and Share the World. She also briefly belonged to a group called Malcolm Locks that released a 1974 album titled Caribbean Rocks.

Liz is originated from Jamaica. She and American singer John Schlitt both became born-again Christians. Her 2004 album, Let It Be, was inspired by her faith.
