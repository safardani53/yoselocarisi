Caroline Quentin shocks The One Show viewers by revealing secret NAKED hobby

CAROLINE Quentin has stunned viewers of The One Show after revealing a rather cheeky side hobby of hers.

During Tuesday evening's instalment of the popular BBC chat show programme, Caroline sat down with hosts Alex Jones and Jermaine Jenas.

The 61-year-old delved into the ins and outs of her new Sky sci-fi series - The Lazarus Project - which predicted the coronavirus pandemic and even the Russian invasion of Ukraine.

Caroline's latest role sees her playing Wes, the head of a highly secretive organisation tasked to prevent worldwide extinction events.

It is also a drama about time travel, with actor Paapa Essiedu playing the lead character, George.

Caroline also revealed a certain hobby of hers includes skinny dipping, leaving the two hosts open-mouthed.

Just last year, Caroline stunned fans after sharing a naked video of herself skinny dipping in a pond.

The Strictly Come Dancing star stripped off completely before hopping into the water at her countryside home.

Caroline was joined by a selection of animals - including a visiting racing pigeon - and admitted it was “really cold” as she swam around.

Her husband Sam Farmer recorded the amusing video of her skinny dipping in the great outdoors.

The footage begins with the Men Behaving Badly and Jonathan Creek star introducing her Instagram followers to her “audience”.
