Photos Of Whitney Houstons Daughter Hollywood Life

Bobbi Kristina Brown, born March 4, 1993, was an American TV personality and the daughter of late singer Whitney Houston and Bobby Brown. Bobbi had a difficult childhood. Born into the spotlight,  she endured unwanted media attention at the same time her parents were struggling with addiction.

Bobbi went on to become a TV personality through series like ‘Being Bobby Brown’ and ‘The Houstons: On Our Own.’ She also dabbled in music, showing promise that she could follow in her famous mother’s footsteps.

Unfortunately, Bobbi suffered a tragic loss when her mother died of an overdose at the age of 48 on February 11, 2012. Roughly three years later, Bobbi would die almost the same way as her mother on July 26, 2015 at the age of 22.  

We’d like to remember Bobbi for her charm and grace. She was glowing at the 2012 premiere party for The Houstons On Our Own. She stunned in sparkles at the event, which took place at New York’s Tribeca Grand hotel.
