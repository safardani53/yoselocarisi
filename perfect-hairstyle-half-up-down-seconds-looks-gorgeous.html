The perfect half up, half down hairstyle takes seconds to do and looks gorgeous on every

A BEAUTY fan has revealed a simple yet clever way to style your hair, which “looks gorgeous” on everyone. 

Matt Newman is a professional hairstylist and often uses his social media accounts to share his top tips and tricks from the industry. 

And after seeing a woman do a half up, half down ‘do, he simply had to try and recreate it for himself. 

Stitching someone else’s video to his own, he first observed the woman twist her hair before using a clip to finish the look. 

“Now who the hell is gonna tell me that on Jacob Elordi's Big Green Earth this isn't the cutest half up hair hack ever?,” the told viewers.

“I have to do it.”

Standing next to a painting, where the sunlight hit him directly, he then went about trying to do it for himself.

Leaving half his hair down, he section two pieces and twist them together before placing one over his head. 

He then took a small clip to secure the middle section of the two strands and moved the front part down over it. 

It created a cute bounce to his mane and the video ended with his asking: “Is it cute?”
