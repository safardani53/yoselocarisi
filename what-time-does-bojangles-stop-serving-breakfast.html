What time does Bojangles' stop serving breakfast?

If you really want to get a taste of the south, and you're in the mood for breakfast food, you're in luck.

Bojangles' Famous Chicken 'n Biscuits is open for breakfast all day.

The restaurant opens most of its locations at 5.30am, seven days a week.

They close at 9pm every day but Saturday when they're open until 10pm.

What are the best breakfast combos at Bojangles'?

The beauty of Bojangles' is that they have a delicious meal waiting for anyone, regardless of what kind of breakfast food you like.

They're famous for their Cajun fried chicken and their biscuits, so it only makes sense to combine them for a Cajun chicken and biscuit sandwich.

You can add cheese and pick from your choice of sides (which are called Fixin's).

These include Bo rounds (like mini hashbrowns) and grits for breakfast.

If you're a vegetarian or have a sweet tooth, you can try Bo-Berry biscuits, which are covered in a sweet glaze.
