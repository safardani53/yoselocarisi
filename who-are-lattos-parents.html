Who are Latto's parents? | The US Sun

LATTO is one of the biggest rising stars on the US rap scene at the moment.

Here's everything you need to know about Latto and her parents.

Who are Latto's parents?

Latto was born to Misti Pitts and Shayne Stephens.

There is not much information about her parents, however, her mother Misti is Caucasian, and her father Shayne is African American.

Latto reportedly stated that she was bullied in school for being bi-racial, thus, she adopted the name Miss Mulatto when she began her music career.

Latto was born on December 22, 1998, in Columbus, Ohio.

However, she moved to Atlanta, Georgia, in her teens.

Latto, real name, Alyssa Michelle Stephens, is a rapper and songwriter who began her professional career in 2016.

She first gained media attention after appearing on Jermaine Dupri's reality television series, The Rap Game, in 2016, where she starred as Miss Mulatto.

Despite her rising success, her mainstream breakthrough was after she released Big Energy, which is the lead single from her second studio album, 777, in 2022.

As a result of her stellar performances, she has won one Georgia Music Award, one BET Award, and one BET Hip Hop Award.

As an entrepreneur, she opened her own store, Pittstop Clothing, in Jonesboro, Georgia.

Is Latto single?

As of the time of writing, Latto is dating.

However, she has decided to keep the name of her boyfriend private.

In Cosmopolitan's July/August cover story, Latto told the publication that she is currently in "the longest relationship I’ve ever been in."

"If something is special to me, I'm going to keep it close to me because everything else has to be front and center about my life," she explained.

"I think we find the beauty in separation sometimes because it makes us miss each other. My privacy is more important [than seeing him in the crowd]. I can't have people putting two and two together."

When talking about trust, she told Cosmopolitan that she is so comfortable with her man that he has allowed her to be in her "soft girl era."

"I think people will probably be caught off guard or shocked at who I am in a relationship versus my public persona," she explained.

"Because people think I’m so tough because I’m super confident and dominant.

"In this relationship at least, I’m way more comfortable with taking the passenger seat and letting him lead because he lets me be myself—and I can still be my alpha self."

Latto has remained quiet about her man's identity but previously told Atlanta radio station Hot 107.9 back in January 2023 that he pays her bills.

While fans search for clues, Latto explained to Cosmopolitan that she loves to cook and he loves to eat.

"He loves when I make food," she said.

"They be trying to crack on me online and he be dead laughing like, 'What food ain’t good?' That man cleans the bone."

What is Latto's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Latto has an estimated net worth of approximately $2million, as of October 2022.
