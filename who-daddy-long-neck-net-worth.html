Who is Daddy Long Neck?

Daddy Long Neck is an American social media personality with over 1.6million followers on Instagram.

Also known as the Long Neck Kid and Damn Long Neck, the star regularly posts self-deprecating content about his condition and skinny appearance.

He first made waves on the internet in 2018 when he collaborated with fellow online star Charles McDowell, popularly known as Wide Neck.

The musical duo created two comedic rap tracks, Neckst Up and Neckst Big Thing, that quickly became viral sensations, generating over eight million views in less than two months.
