Kevin &amp; Danielle Jonas Welcome Baby Girl!

It’s a girl for Kevin and Danielle Jonas!

The former boy band member and his wife welcomed their second child today.

Kevin’s brother Nick shared the news with fans via Instagram:

“Ah! I’m an uncle again!” Nick wrote.

“Congrats Kevin and Dani! We have another Jonas!! Can’t wait to meet that beautiful baby girl very soon.”

Nick is currently in Hawaii filming Jumanji with Dwayne Johnson and Jack Black.

Kevin has yet to confirm the news himself, but yesterday, he informed fans that he was expecting to become a father of two any minute:

“Waiting is the hardest part,” he tweeted.

“For me but Dani would probably say the contractions hahahaha.”

Kevin and Danielle got married back in 2009, when he was just 22 years old.

They met while on vacation in the Bahamas with their families two years earlier.

In 2014, the couple welcomed their first child, a girl named Alena Rose.

Kevin and Danielle starred in the E! reality series Married to Jonas from 2012 to 2013.

Yeah, it seems E! execs may have overestimated the level of public interest in Kevin Jonas’ love life.

The Jonases have enjoyed sharing pregnancy updates with their fans via Instagram.

First, there was the above announcement, a fairly clever way of going public with the news.

Then there was this pic teasing the reveal of the baby’s gender:

Clearly, Kevin’s dad-joke sense of humor is on point.

And friends say he’s not too shabby at the other aspects of parenting, either.

Kevin has mostly left the limelight behind in favor of focusing on his family.

We’re sure he’s still sitting on a pretty sizable pile of Disney cash.
