A TEEN Mom star has revealed her true feelings about reality TV and why she would never return to the small screen.

Teen Mom 2 alum, Vee Rivera, opened up about her time on the franchise on the latest installment of her podcast, Vibin' & Kinda Thrivin'.

During Monday's episode, Vee and her co-host, Alessandra Gonzalez, dedicated the hour to answering listeners' burning questions about the pals.

However, one response, in particular, gave fans more than they bargained for when Vee ended up ranting over her reality TV days.

Alessandra first read the question: "Would Vee ever do another reality show, and if so, would she do it with Alessandra?"

Vee immediately fired back, saying she would never return to filming due to her previous experiences on Teen Mom.

The 31-year-old appeared on numerous episodes of Teen Mom 2 as the wife of Jo Rivera - Kailyn Lowry's first baby daddy.

The pair welcomed their daughter, Vivi, two years earlier, joining Jo and Kailyn's 12-year-old son, Isaac.

In April 2022, Kailyn, 30, decided to leave the Teen Mom franchise, prompting the rest of the family to follow suit.

But the decision seemed good for the family as Vee claimed the kids weren't too fond of the cameras.

"That's another reason why [I wouldn't return to reality TV]. The kids, I would never put them in that - Isaac already hates it. He loves that we're not on the show anymore, so I would never bring that back up. And for Vivi, I never wanted her on the show anyway."

NEW CAREER MOVE?

Alessandra later turned the tables and asked her co-host if she would consider another route in showbiz.

The New Jersey native asked: "Would you act? Would you ever be in a TV show?" 

Vee enthusiastically responded: "Yes, I was just talking to Jo about this the other day because he said I would be great in a Novella. I was like, I would love to be in a Novella.

"Maybe next year I'll apply for some acting positions. Maybe I can get in a movie or something. I would love to act."

Since leaving the small screen, Vee has put her efforts toward her podcasts, including joining forces with Kailyn for a weekly podcast, Baby Mamas No Drama.

DELAWARE MANSION

The real estate agent also regularly shares photos of her life on social media and has updated fans on the renovation of her new $650,000 Delaware pad.

The U.S. Sun exclusively reported that Vee and Jo purchased a four-bedroom property in July, though they haven't moved in yet as it is currently under construction.

In September, Vee showed off the progress of the renovations on her Instagram Stories, explaining that they ripped out the interior of the 3,911 square-foot home and planned to refurbish it completely.

The spacious abode includes a massive kitchen and bar space and a large brick fireplace in the living room.

It also has a small workout room and a game room with a big pool table on the ground floor.

Off of their enormous kitchen, two double window doors slide open to reveal a lovely furnished patio.

The patio opens into their sizable backyard, which includes an extra buildable lot, two-car garage and basketball court.
