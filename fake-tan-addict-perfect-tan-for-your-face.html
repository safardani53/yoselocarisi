Im a fake tan addict and have found the perfect tan for your face - its only 3.99

SELF-TANNING can be difficult, especially when it comes to doing your face.

But now one tanning addict has shared her £3.99 discovery that is perfect for tanning your face.

Posting to TikTok, Southerly Bower, says that he’s been fake tanning since she was in secondary school, but has always struggled with finding products for her face.

She says: “You should never use a body tan on your face. It will block your pores, it will make you spotty. You always need to separate your body tan and your face tan.”

Southerly say’s there’s been once face tan that she’s been using for a couple of years now - but is rather expensive, so she’s been looking for a new option.

Sharing her newest tanning discovery she says: “I met a friend at work and was like ‘oh my god you’re so glowy’ and she was like ‘yeah I just used this Garnier face mist.’”

The face mist in question is the Garnier Ambre Solaire natural self tan face mist.

Southerly says that since trying this face mist she’s been very impressed, adding that: “It doesn’t go on my pillow. I have white bedding and it’s never stained, it doesn’t smell, doesn’t block my pores and hasn’t made me break out.”

When asked how long it lasts, Southerly says: “I spray it on a Monday and it lasts a good week but top up might be needed depending on skin type and products in your skin care regime!”
