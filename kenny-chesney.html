Kenny Chesney Biography

Kenny Chesney was born Kenneth Arnold Chesney on March 26, 1968, in Knoxville, Tennessee, USA. As of now, he is 55 years old and of American nationality. Similarly, he is a Christian who was born under the sign of Aries.

In terms of his family, he was born to David Chesney and Karen Chandler. David, his father, was an elementary school teacher, and Karen, his mother, was a hairstylist. Furthermore, he has a younger sister named Jennifer Chandler. In terms of education, he attended Gibbs High School and graduated in 1986. He aspired to be a star athlete while in school. However, he did attend East Tennessee State University to study advertising after graduating from high school. He received his diploma in 1990 from this institution.

Career

Capricorn Records discovered Kenny at a songwriters’ show in 1993. In 1994, Capricorn Records released his debut album, ‘My Wildest Dream.’ On June 13, 1995, BNA Records released Kenny’s second album, “All I Need to Know.” In 1996, Kenny released ‘Me and You.’ ‘I Will Stand’ was released in 1997. On September 26, 2000, Kenny released his greatest hits compilation album.

Chesney’s two albums released in 2005 were ‘Be As You Are: Songs From an Old Blue Chair’ and ‘The Road And The Radio.’ On November 15, 2007, he released another compilation album, ‘Super Hits.’ The ‘The Flip Flop Summer Tour’ was also held this year. Chesney released his new album, ‘Lucky Old Sun,’ on October 14, 2008. In April 2009, he began his ‘Sun City Carnival Tour,’ which began in Uncasville, Connecticut.

‘Hemmingway Whiskey,’ Cheney’s new CD, was released in 2010. The film’s title was ‘The Color Orange,’ and it premiered on ESPN on February 20, 2011. Chesney’s thirteenth studio album, ‘Welcome to Fish Bowl,’ was released in 2012. They performed in 22 different cities, beginning in Tampa and ending in Foxboro at the end of August 2012. The tour attracted over a million people and generated $96 million in revenue.

In March 2013, Chesney began his thirteenth tour, the ‘No Shoes Nation Tour.’ It was held to promote his most recent album, “Life on Rock,” which came out on February 5, 2013. ‘The Big Revival,’ Chesney’s most recent album, was released on September 23, 2014. It was a smash hit, with many of its songs reaching the top of the Country Airplay Chart. He embarked on ‘The Big Revival Tour’ the following year. It began on March 26, 2015, in Nashville and ended in Foxboro.

Kenny Chesney’s Net Worth

As a professional singer from the United States, he has undoubtedly amassed a sizable fortune. He is estimated to have a net worth of $200 million US dollars as of August 2023.

Kenny Chesney Wife, Marriage

Since 2012, Mary Nolan has been Kenny Chesney’s long-term girlfriend. The couple has appeared together in public at events such as the CMA Awards and the Country Countdown Awards. On May 9, 2005, he married actress Renée Zellweger on the U.S. Virgin Islands resort island of St. John. Their marriage, however, was short-lived, as they divorced in September 2005. Among the women he dated were Adrianna Costa, Jamie Hill Fuller, Amy Colley, and Jenn Brown.
