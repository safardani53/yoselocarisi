EastEnders' Danny Walters returns to social media with two word message for fans after Keanu Taylor'

EASTENDERS star Danny Walters has returned to social media with a short but sweet post after his character's shock death.

Last night fans were lost for words when it was revealed that Linda Carter killed Keanu Taylor in the flash forward episode of the BBC soap.

Over the past 10 months EastEnders viewers were desperate to know who the mystery male surrounded by The Six were in the Queen Vic.

Earlier today Danny took to his Instagram to break his silence after the epic instalment of the show.

Danny shared a snap of her alter-ego on the platform and simply captioned the post: "Keanu Taylor."

Fans and co-stars rushed to the comments section to share their love and support.

Claire Norris, who plays Bernadette Taylor in EastEnders, wrote some sad face and love heart emojis.

Jay Brown star Jamie Borthwick said: "My man."

The flash forward episode saw Sharon Watts, Linda Carter, Denise Fox, Suki Panesar, Stacey Slater and Kathy Beale stand around a dead body earlier this year.
