I heard the clothes at George at Asda were popping so went to see if the rumours were true - this

A FASHION fan did a huge £200 haul at Asda to see if their latest clothing range lived up to the hype - and it seems there are some must-have pieces if you’re stocking up for Spring. 

With the weather finally turning warmer, Regan decided to check out some of George’s latest swimwear, dresses and loungewear sets. 

And it seems it was a successful trip, with the keen bargain hunter finding a dress she claims will be perfect for summer evenings - and it’s only £18. 

Known online as @reganellisx, she regularly shares bargain fashion hauls with her 207,200 followers. 

In a new reel, she said: “Right, I have heard that George at Asda are doing some banging clothes at the minute, so I’m going to go down there now and see what they’ve got. 

“Also, I’ve heard that their summer stuff has started coming in and I’ve got a load of holidays coming up, so I wanted to try their swimwear as someone who’s got big boobs but no arse.
