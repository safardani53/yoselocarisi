This Goat Is Ready for Warmer Weather

In between remixes of Taylor Swift songs, goats like to relax, and one of the best ways to do this is by sitting in the sun and eating a nice book.

That’s why the Nigerian dwarf goat in this video is ecstatic that the weather is finally warming up. Now that spring is in full gear, Peppa Lass is getting out of her Tennessee home to enjoy the great outdoors – or at least her backyard.

In this clip, the adorable farm animal shows off some of her favorite spring/summer activities, including swinging, going to the beach and playing tag with her friends.

Peppa Lass also knows how to rest and relax in style. Check out that fun and flirty pink dress!
