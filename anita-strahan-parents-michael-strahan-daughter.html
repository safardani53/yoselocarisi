Who is Tanita Strahan? | The US Sun

TANITA Strahan has built a career as an artist.

She is the eldest child of television personality and former sports star, Michael Strahan.

Who is Tanita Strahan?

Tanita Strahan was born in Germany on November 10, 1992, as per Sportskeeda.

The 31-year-old is the daughter of former NFL player Michael and his ex-wife, Wanda Hutchins.

Michael and Wanda were married from 1992 to 1996 and welcomed two kids together, including Tanita and 28-year-old Michael Strahan Jr.

According to People, Tanita resided in Houston, Texas until her parents' divorce.

The outlet reported that she and Michael Jr. moved to Germany to live with their mother following the split.

In 2016, Tanita spoke with People about her experience growing up in Germany, saying: "I've never been the type of person to be starstruck. Growing up in Germany, no one cared about stuff like that."

Tanita went back to Houston for high school before earning a degree in design and visual communication from the Fashion Institute of Design and Merchandising in Los Angeles, California.

As per the publication, she later returned to Texas to study art.

What does Tanita Strahan do for work?

Tanita works as a visual artist.

According to her online gallery, Tanita "has worked with various mediums," such as:

  • Acrylic
  • Watercolor
  • Gouache
  • Ink
  • Graphic Design (Illustrator and Photoshop)
  • Photography

Tanita frequently shares her work on her Instagram page, @tanitaa.st, where she has over 8.5K followers.

Michael has supported his daughter's career by sharing information about her artwork on Instagram.

On July 12, 2021, the Good Morning America co-anchor posted an image of one of her paintings, along with the caption: "Calling all art lovers! My talented daughter, @tanitaa.st, art is on sale TODAY!! Hit the link in my bio or go to Tanitagallery.com to get yours! #ProudDad."
