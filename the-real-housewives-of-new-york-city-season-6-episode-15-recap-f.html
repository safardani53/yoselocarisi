Fear &amp; Boring in Montana

Last night on The Real Housewives of New York City Season 6 Episode 15, the ladies broke out the cowboy boots, because they’re headed to Montana!

The only absence was Aviva Drescher, who came down with asthma and "was not allowed" to travel to such a high altitude at the last moment.

In other words, Aviva Drescher was "fired" by Bravo again.

Anyway, for those who did go, the trip started out like we’ve seen countless times before, with the women drinking and whining about where they are.

A place that just happens to be spectacular, obviously.

Then the real bickering began as LuAnn De Lesseps straight up asked Sonja Morgan why her facialist was gossiping about herself and Carole Radziwill.

 

It’s a fair question, one that tied into the rumor that Sonja slept with Russ, which she obviously doesn’t want to deny as Morgan loves the attention.

She eventually acted like she did not bone Russ when Carole said she called him to ask, which contradicts her behavior a week ago, but whatever.

That wasn’t enough to calm LuAnn down, though.

Unappeaesed, the former Countess started freaking out on Sonja for even using this facial-giving woman who loves her celebrity gossip so hardcore.

Morgan was not at all turned off. After all, if you watch The Real Housewives of New York City online, you know you’re no one if you aren’t talked about.

Or is that Gossip Girl? We forgot.

It was actually a pretty boring episode at the end of the day, though next week may up the ante as LuAnn apparently decides to berate Sonja harder over this.

Here’s hoping.
