The DJs put on a sensory extravaganza of entrancing light shows and lasers, all backed by a world-class sound system within the repurposed confines of the former Ikea warehouse.

The biggest name in UK dance had the 15,000-strong crowd captivated by their infectious energy and pulsating beats.

Every track merged seamlessly with the one before, creating a journey for the head and heart.
