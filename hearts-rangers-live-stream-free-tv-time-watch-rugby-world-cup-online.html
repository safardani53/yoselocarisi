Hearts vs Rangers: TV channel, live stream, teams and kick off time for Scottish Premiership The

RANGERS are return to action as they head to Edinburgh to take on a badly out-of-form Hearts.

Steven Gerrard's side have won four Scottish Premiership games on the spin and went top of the table before the international break.

What time is kick-off?

You can catch this game live on Sunday, October 20.

Kick-off is at 12.15pm.

When the sides last met here, Rangers ran out 3-1 winners back in April.

Which TV channel and live stream can I watch Hearts vs Rangers on?

You can catch this one live on BT Sport 1 and BT Sport Ultimate.
Coverage begins at midday.

If you want to stream, download the official BT Sport app on your games console, mobile, tablet or PC.

Can I watch Rangers vs Hearts for free?

Viewers with an EE phone contract can get a free trial of BT Sport for 3 months.
