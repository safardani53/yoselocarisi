Who is David Benavidez:

David Benavidez is a famous Boxer. He was born on December 17, 1996 and his birthplace is Phoenix, AZ. David is also well known as, American professional boxer who leaped to the top of his sport when he took home the WBC super middleweight title in September 2017. Out of his first 21 pro victories, 18 of them were by knockout.

David is originated from United States. He fought on the undercard of the Victor Ortiz vs. Andre Berto bout in April 2016.
