Who is Doug Pinnick:

Doug Pinnick is a famous Rock Singer. He was born on September 3, 1950 and his birthplace is Illinois. Doug is also well known as, Illinois-born musician best known as the bassist and vocalist for the progressive hard rock band King’s X. He has become one of the most respected singers in rock, making guest appearances on songs by bands like Pearl Jam and Dream Theater.

Doug is originated from United States. He has been bandmates with drummer Jerry Gaskill in King’s X.
