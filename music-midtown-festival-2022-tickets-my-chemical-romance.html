Music Midtown Festival 2022: Where and when can I buy tickets?

A SLEW of performers is gearing up to take the stage at another musical festival just in time for Summer.

The 2022 Music Midtown Festival features a noteworthy roster of music artists, which is why fans are curious to know how to get tickets to the spectacle.

Where can I buy tickets to Music Midtown Festival 2022?

Through social media, the Music Midtown Festival teased fans with what's to come for this year's edition of the famed event.

On Twitter, they announced the concert dates and tweeted: "You know what to do! Mark your calendars for Music Midtown 2022 on Saturday & Sunday, September 17 + 18 #MusicMidtown."

On the aforementioned dates, the festival will take place at Piedmont Park in Atlanta, Georgia.

From Wednesday, May 11 to Monday, May 16 those who wished to attend the series of concerts were able to purchase early bird tickets through Midtown's official website.

General admission goes on sale on Friday, May 20, and includes a layaway plan, as well as a $25 cashback opportunity if the attendee used PayPal to purchase.

VIP packages and other types of admission are also available through the official Midtown site.

Previously, the spectacle experienced complications due to the Covid-19 pandemic and was not able to hold a 2020 edition of Music Midtown.

Although they did hold a 2021 version of the concert series, the company reassured music fans that their canceled tickets from 2020 and 2021 will be honored.

They tweeted: "If you have rollover tickets from 2020 or 2021, you don't need to take any action."

"Your tickets are good! You should have an email from Front Gate Tickets confirming your order."

Who is in the lineup?

On May 17, 2022, Music Midtown revealed its complete festival lineup.

Days before on May 11, they teased prospective attendees with one highly recognizable name.

My Chemical Romance

On May 11, the company took to Twitter to let music fans know that "You already know one of the 2022 headliners! @MCRofficial will be at Music Midtown this fall."

My Chemical Romance is fronted by lead vocalist, Gerard Way, and consists of other group members Ray Toro, Frank Lero, and Mikey Way.

The rock music group found success during the 2000s and officially broke up in March 2013.

In 2019, My Chemical Romance declared their intentions to reunite in the following years.

In 2022, their headlining their own tour and are also performing at the When We Were Young Festival.

Phoebe Bridgers

Formerly a member of Sloppy Jane, Phoebe Bridgers' solo career gained traction in 2017.

Her first solo studio album, Stranger in the Alps, was released in September 2017.

In addition to the Music Midtown Festival, she is also embarking on her Phoebe Bridgers Reunion Tour in 2022.

Future

Fresh off of his ninth studio album release, hip-hop hitmaker Future is also among the lineup for Music Midtown 2022.

He garnered success in the 2010s for his popular singles Turn On The Lights, Mask Off, Draco, and the Drake-assisted What A Time To Be Alive.

Future has been nominated for several awards, including Billboard Music Awards, Grammys, and the MTV Video Music Awards.

Fall Out Boy

Acclaimed rock band Fall Out Boy is also setting foot on stage to bring their hits live to festivalgoers in Atlanta.

Similar to MCR, the group broke through the mainstream during the early 2000s decade.

FOB is fronted by Patrick Stump and consists of members Pete Wentz, Joe Trohman, and Andy Hurley.

Their popular hits include Alone Together, Thnks fr th Mmrs, and Sugar, We're Going Down.
