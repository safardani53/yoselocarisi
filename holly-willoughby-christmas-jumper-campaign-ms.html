Holly Willoughby looks incredible in Christmas jumpers for sparkly new M&amp;S campaign

HOLLY Willoughby looks absolutely incredible in her Christmas jumper posing for a sparkly new M&S campaign.

The This Morning presenter, 39, is the face of shop's festive adverts - and the clothes look perfect for lounging around in considering the chilly weather that has hit the UK this week.

Despite only being the beginning of November, Holly has officially marked the beginning of the festive holidays by modelling some new bits from Marks & Spencer.

The telly favourite looked like a natural behind the camera as she showed off the new range whilst posing in a Christmas themed room, surrounded by red and gold baubles.

Holly showed us that she can be versatile with her looks and posed for one of the snaps in a Fair Isle knit, some black mom jeans and chunky black boots.

Yet in another, she showed off her more glam side with a sparkling black midi skirt - which perfectly showed off her gorgeous hourglass figure - paired with a cream cashmere jumper and some black pointed toe heels.

Holly was all smiles as the camera candidly caught her giggling away to herself in a cream and navy polka dot jumper, which was styled with a pair of navy blue jeans.

She also put on a huge grin as she looked into the distance whilst modelling a black and white aztec patterned jumper and black jeans.

The presenter looked gorgeous as ever with her shiny blonde locks styled into loose curls and a middle parting.

She completed each look with natural looking make-up, which consisted of a glossy pink lip, arched brows and popping highlight.
