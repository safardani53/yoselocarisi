Makeovers On Kim Kardashian &amp; More Hollywood Life

Celebs are always out and about in nothing but style from head to toe. Whether they are sporting their designer tees, signature shades, or custom made sneakers, they are surely killing it in the fashion game.

Just one more thing celebs seem to be rocking are cool, colorful haircuts. That’s right, changing the mane seems to be a trend many celebs have picked up on rather recently.     

Doing your hair can most certainly become quite the fun and creative activity, as you can do many different things with it. You can cut it, curl it, straighten it, fringe it… and, oh yeah, color it! 

Celebs who always seem to enjoy a little pop of color with their fresh hairdos (particularly the color silver) include Rita Ora, Lady Gaga, and Kelly Osbourne.
