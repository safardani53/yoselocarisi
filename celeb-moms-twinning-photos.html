Celeb Moms and Daughters, Twinning Photos

Gabrielle Union & Kaavia James

Hawaiian print shirts are back, and the L.A.'s Finest actress and her baby girl just single-handedly proved why in these matching sets. Beyond serving up looks, we're grateful for their subtle reminder to stay hydrated this summer.

Emily Ratajkowski & Sly

It's not just moms and daughters who love a matching moment! The model shared a series of photos of herself and her baby, whom she welcomed with husband Sebastian Bear-McClard on March 8, matching in blue and orange bathing suits. "Bday eve with the dream vacation partner," she captioned the colorful shots.

Bethenny & Bryn

Powerful in pink! The entrepreneur and her 11-year-old daughter Bryn Hoppy donned lookalike ensembles, right down to Bryn's baby Birkin.

Chrissy & Luna

The lookalike mommy-daughter duo wore coordinated pink tropical looks - and posed like flamingos to complete the theme.

Larsa & Sophia

Larsa Pippen posted an Instagram wearing matching butterfly-print bikinis with her daughter Sophia, with the caption, "Twinning 🦋 bikini: @whitefoxboutique."

In the photo, Larsa is sporting a classic triangle style teamed with a sheer wrap and oversized black sunglasses. Sophia strikes the same pose as her famous mom in a similar tie-front top, high-waisted bottoms and '90s-inspired shades.

Kylie & Stormi

The Kylie Cosmetics mogul takes the words right out of our mouths with her Instagram caption: "My baby!! I can't handle this!!" To kick off the Halloween 2019 festivities, Stormi recreates her mom's iconic Met Gala look from 2019, purple plumage and all.

Gabrielle & Kaavia

This mother-daughter duo knows how to "bring it!" Ahead of Halloween, Union and Kaavia James twin in matching cheerleader costumes inspired by the America's Got Talent judge's beloved 2000 movie Bring It On.

Kris & Kylie

The jet-setting matriarch and her beauty mogul daughter seem to have met their match when it comes to living in luxury. The pair look ultra-chic in nearly identical all-black ensembles, oversized black sunglasses and glimmering diamond necklaces - not to mention their matching ultra-rare Hermès Himalayan Crocodile Kelly handbags, valued at more than $140,000.

Serena & Olympia

Mom Serena and baby girl Alexis Olympia model their matching pink bathing suits for the camera.

The tennis star's one-piece says, "the shady one," while her little one sports a mini version labeled, "wild child."

Khloé & True

"A leopard and her cub 🐆," Khloé writes alongside this fashionably cute photo of her and her baby girl True.

Kate & Rani

Mama Kate Hudson and her adorable daughter Rani Rose are all smiles in their matching sundresses.

Hoda & Haley

The Today anchor and her oldest daughter make a splash at the beach in matching Lilly Pulitzer swimsuits, courtesy of Kotb's own mother.

Khloé, True, Kylie & Stormi

For the Kardashian-Jenner crew's annual Christmas Eve bash in 2018, Khloé and Kylie go for a maximalist approach with daughters True and Stormi in their festive mommy-and-me outfits.

Lauren, Ada & Willa

Lauren Akins, husband Thomas Rhett and their girls - Ada James and Willa Gray - wear matching swimsuits during their family beach outing.

Mariah & Monroe

Mariah Carey and daughter Monroe slip into moto jackets and wear matching sunglasses at the 2018 Kids Choice Awards.

Coco & Ioni

Ioni follows in her supermodel mom Coco Rocha's footsteps - as the pair struts their stuff during Paris Fashion Week.

Hilaria & Carmen

Photographic evidence Alec Baldwin's leading ladies - Hilaria and Carmen - are each other's style inspiration, on the set of a shoot for Janie and Jack.

Jenna & Everly

We see Jenna Dewan acting as one chill mermaid in plain sight ... as daughter Everly can be seen in the corner, with her feet adorably popping out of her tail.
