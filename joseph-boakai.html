Who is Joseph Boakai:

Joseph Boakai is a famous Politician. He was born on November 30, 1944 and his birthplace is Liberia. Joseph is also well known as, Liberian politician who became Vice President of Liberia in January of 2006. He previously served as the Minister of Agriculture from 1983 to 1985.

Joseph is originated from Liberia. He served under President Ellen Johnson.
