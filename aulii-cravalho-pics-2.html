Aulii Cravalho Pics Hollywood Life

Auli’i Cravalho, born Nov. 22 2000, is an actress and singer best known for her voiceover work. The young actress rose to fame in 2016 when she earned the role of the titular character in Disney’s animated film ‘Moana.’ Auli’i not only did her own voice acting, she sang for the role, too! The role led her to a live performance of the Oscar-nominated song ‘How Far I’ll Go’ at the 2017 Oscars where she stunned audiences. After the success of ‘Moana,’ Auli’i starred in the ensemble of the short-lived series ‘Rise.’ 2019 saw the young talent take on another Disney heroine when she played the role of Ariel in ‘The Little Mermaid Live!’ musical event on ABC.
