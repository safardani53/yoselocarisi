Jasmin Walia reveals her despair with fans as airport strike leaves her stranded in Greece The S

JASMIN Walia revealed she's getting a longer holiday than she planned for as airport strikes have left her stranded in Greece.

The former TOWIE star revealed the news to fans on Twitter as she wrote: "Our flights have been cancelled because of an air strike ...how we gonna get home tomo(sic)."

She then shared a screenshot of the text message she had received from Easy Jet which explained that her flight would no longer be departing from the airport.

The message stated: "We're very sorry, due to a Greek air traffic control s trike your flight has been cancelled. To transfer onto a new flight or obtain a refund you can use our Flight Tracker: easyjet.com/5156. We apologise for the inconvenience caused."

Jasmin pleaded: "Can you please follow me so I can DM you I need to get home and got this message @easyJet."

Prior to the surprise flight cancellations, Jasmin had been treated her followers to an array of idyllic snaps from the sunshine break.

The 26-year-old's holiday comes a few months after The Sun revealed her two year relationship with Ross Worswick had ended.

Jasmin and Ross, who shot to fame in Ex On The Beach, have been dating since April 2014 but decided to call it a day after two years together.

A source tells The Sun: “Ross and Jasmine have broken up.

“Ross is devastated. He changed his life for her and moved down south.”

The pair last split in April 2015 when Jasmin told the Daily Star: “[Ross is] a nice guy but he wasn’t very supportive of my career and my schedule. There was a strain on the relationship.”

However they got back together for over a year and continued to look loved-up and happy on nights out together.

The pair appeared on Sky 1 reality show Desi Rascals together where their relationship was at times strained.
